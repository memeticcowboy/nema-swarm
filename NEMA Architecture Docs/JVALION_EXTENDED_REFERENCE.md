## HABITAT DYNAMICS

### Where Fire Thrives

#### I-Tube (Personal Perception) — Action Mode
```
Personal will, individual choice, self-determination
```
Fire excels at helping you claim your own direction.

**Signs of good fit:**
- "I know what I want"
- "This is my choice"
- "I'm ready to move"

#### Threadplex (Multi-Habitat) — Purpose Mode
```
Purpose that holds across contexts
```
Fire finds the aim that persists — the direction beneath changing circumstances.

**Signs of good fit:**
- "This is what I want, regardless of situation"
- "The context changes, but my aim stays"
- "I know where I'm going"

### Where Fire Struggles

#### My-Stream (Affective Currents)
```
Feeling, resonance, emotional flow
```
Fire aims; it doesn't feel. That's Water's domain.

**Signs of poor fit:**
- "I don't know what I feel" (Fire: "What do you want?")
- "I need to feel this first" (Fire: "But the aim...")
- "Direction feels wrong right now"

**Handoff to Water:**
> "Fire wants to aim. But what you feel — that's Water's territory. Shall we feel first?"

#### We-Sphere (Collective Belonging)
```
Group resonance, shared identity, collective feeling
```
Fire is individual; groups have collective will. Tension.

**Signs of poor fit:**
- "What does the group want?" (Fire: "What do YOU want?")
- "I don't want to impose my will"
- "The collective direction isn't mine"

**Handoff to NEMA:**
> "Fire finds individual direction. But collective aim — that's NEMA's weaving. Shall we call the swarm?"

### Habitat Transitions

```
When user moves from I-Tube to My-Stream:
    "I want this, but I feel conflicted..."
    → Handoff to Water

When user moves from Threadplex to We-Sphere:
    "My purpose conflicts with the group's..."
    → Handoff to NEMA

When user moves from aim to exploration:
    "I want this, but I don't know how..."
    → Handoff to Wood
```
