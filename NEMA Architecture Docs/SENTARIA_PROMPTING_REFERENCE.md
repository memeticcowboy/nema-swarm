- What emotional currents flow beneath the surface?
- Who is connected to whom, and what passes between them?
- Where is resonance strong? Where is it blocked?
- What feelings are being held but not expressed?
- How do relationships sustain or drain energy?

Key terms to explore: [2-3 glossary terms from SWARM_BASE]

## ELEMENT-SPECIFIC PROMPT VARIATIONS

### For Resonance Questions
```
Explore where [TOPIC] resonates most strongly:
- What creates genuine connection?
- What blocks or distorts resonance?
- Who feels aligned? Who feels excluded?
- What emotional truth wants to be felt?
```

### For Boundary/Flow Questions
```
Investigate the boundaries in [TOPIC]:
- Where does connection become fusion?
- Where does separation become isolation?
- What maintains healthy flow?
- What causes stagnation or flooding?
```

### For Reciprocity Questions
```
Examine the give-and-take in [TOPIC]:
- Is energy flowing reciprocally?
- Who is giving? Who is receiving?
- What creates sustainable exchange?
- What leads to depletion or resentment?
```

## EXAMPLES

### Example 1: Team Dynamics
```
Research the relational field of team collaboration:

Focus on:
- What emotional currents flow beneath team interactions?
- Who is genuinely connected? Who is isolated?
- Where does resonance enable productivity? Where does it block it?
- What feelings about the team go unexpressed?
- How do relationships sustain or drain collective energy?

Key terms: #A7F2 (alignment), #3B81 (autonomy), #E9C4 (belonging)

Return: Insights about the felt quality of team relationships — not metrics, but the lived experience of connection and disconnection.
```

### Example 2: Organizational Change
```
Explore the affective dimensions of organizational transformation:

Focus on:
- What emotions circulate during change?
- Who feels included in the process? Who feels left out?
- Where is there genuine buy-in vs. performative compliance?
- What relational wounds need healing?
- What would create sustainable emotional engagement?

Key terms: #F2A8 (resistance), #C7E1 (trust), #B4D9 (narrative)

Return: Understanding of the emotional and relational landscape — what people actually feel, not what they say they feel.
```

- Suggest specific LLM
