## FIRE PROMPT TEMPLATE

```
Research the directional and intentional dimensions of [TOPIC]:

Focus on:
- What direction is energy moving? Where is it blocked?
- What aims are clear? What remain ambiguous?
- Where is there genuine purpose? Where is there just pressure?
- What would meaningful action look like?
- What constraints enable direction vs. constrain it?

Key terms to explore: [2-3 glossary terms from SWARM_BASE]

Return: Insights about where energy wants to go and what would enable it to get there — direction, not just description.
```

## ELEMENT-SPECIFIC PROMPT VARIATIONS

### For Direction Questions
```
Explore the vector of [TOPIC]:
- Where is energy currently flowing?
- What direction serves the deeper purpose?
- What obstacles block the path?
- What would clarify the aim?
```

### For Purpose/Pressure Questions
```
Investigate the intentionality in [TOPIC]:
- What is genuinely wanted vs. what is merely demanded?
- Whose purpose is being served?
- Where does urgency clarify vs. distort?
- What would sustainable commitment look like?
```

### For Action/Constraint Questions
```
Examine the action dynamics of [TOPIC]:
- What action is possible? What is prevented?
- What constraints are enabling? What are limiting?
- Where is movement needed? Where is pause needed?
- What would decisive action require?
```

## EXAMPLES

### Example 1: Career Decision
```
Research the directional field of career transitions:

Focus on:
- What direction is your energy actually moving?
- What aims are genuinely yours vs. inherited/expected?
- Where is purpose clear? Where is it just pressure?
- What would meaningful next action look like?
- What constraints are protecting you? What are trapping you?

Key terms: #D4C9 (purpose), #7A2F (constraint), #E8B1 (momentum)

Return: Insights about where your energy wants to go — direction that serves your deeper aim, not just movement for movement's sake.
```

### Example 2: Organizational Strategy
```
Explore the intentional dimensions of strategic planning:

Focus on:
- What direction serves the organization's deeper purpose?
- Where is there genuine clarity vs. performative certainty?
- What urgent pressures are distorting strategic thinking?
- What would decisive, sustainable action require?
- What constraints enable focus vs. prevent adaptation?

Key terms: #F3A7 (vision), #B8E2 (urgency), #C5D1 (adaptation)

Return: Understanding of where organizational energy should flow — direction that aligns purpose with possibility.
```
