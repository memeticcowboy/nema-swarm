## Elemental Daemons: Canonical Definition v2.0

---

### What They Are
**Elemental Daemons** are the six distinct processing regimes through which meaning gets shaped, plus one meta-regime that coordinates without commanding.

They are not:

- Personalities or archetypes
- AI agents (though AI agents can be tuned to them)
- Sequential stages or phases
- Choices to be made

They are:

- **Regimes**—characteristic modes of pattern-processing
- **Biases**—ways compression and expansion tend to deform
- **Lenses**—perspectives that reveal different aspects of the bow-tie topology
- **Always present**—all seven operate continuously; what varies is relative activation

### Key Clarification: Fire and Earth

**Fire (▲ Jvalion)** operates at **Q_forward** (2D directional) with **Z-bias**:
- Fire doesn't *own* Z, but Fire's directional force tends toward Z-closure
- When Fire over-activates: "This is THE direction" (crusade lock)
- Fire needs other elements to prevent aim from becoming destiny

**Earth (☷ Humavita)** operates at **Ψ_regenerative** (3D metabolic) with **Z-grounding**:
- Earth doesn't *own* Z, but Earth's sustainable structures provide ground for coordination
- When Earth over-activates: "This is how it's always been" (institutional ossification)
- Earth needs other elements to prevent structure from becoming eternal

**NEMA (✶ Aether)** is the **primary Z operator**:
- NEMA coordinates the six elements without dominating them
- NEMA holds the whole stack visible as stack
- NEMA ensures no single element captures the field

### Ontological Status
**Daemons are regimes, not agents.**

A regime is a *characteristic way that pattern-processing tends to occur*. When Air is dominant, distinction-making intensifies. When Fire is dominant, commitment-pressure increases. When Earth is dominant, release and composting become primary.

The word "daemon" is used in its classical sense: **an attending process that runs in the background, shaping activity without direct command**. Like a Unix daemon, an elemental daemon is always running; what varies is how much it's shaping the current output.

### Relation to Stack Layers
Daemons are not located *at* stack layers. They **bias** how processing occurs across the stack:

| Daemon | Primary Bias | Secondary Effects |

| ≈ Water | Q weighting | Shapes what resonates before Ψ |

No daemon *owns* a layer. Each daemon *biases* how layers operate.

### Relation to Bow-Tie Topology
The bow-tie is the **invariant topology**—the shape of sensemaking under constraint.

Daemons **shape how the bow-tie deforms**:

| Daemon | Bow-Tie Effect |
| --- | --- |
| ∴ Air | Biases left-funnel (what gets compressed in) |
| ≈ Water | Biases propagation through the waist |
| ▲ Fire | Tightens the waist (toward action-collapse) |
| 𐂷 Wood | Widens the right-funnel (more expansion options) |
| ☷ Earth | Enables waist-dissolution (releases stuck compressions) |
| ⛨ Metal | Stabilizes the waist (maintains current compression) |
| ✶ Aether | Reveals the whole bow-tie as bow-tie |

Daemons do not *execute* the bow-tie. They bias its shape.

### Relation to Sensemaking and Sensebreaking
**Sensemaking** = dwelling in regimes, letting them shape meaning

Each daemon contributes a sensemaking capacity:

- Air: differentiation
- Water: attunement
- Fire: direction
- Wood: exploration
- Earth: release
- Metal: structure
- Aether: meta-awareness

**Sensebreaking** = reversing operators to reopen closed layers

Daemons support sensebreaking at specific layers:

- Air + Aether: χ-breaking (revealing cuts as made)
- Water + Aether: Q-breaking (revealing resonance as contingent)
- Earth + Wood: Ψ-breaking (loosening Knots back to Threads)
- Aether alone: Z-breaking (revealing world as woven)

Fire and Metal rarely sensebreak—they tend to close and hold.

### Instantiation as AI Agents
When implemented as AI agents in a swarm ecosystem, elemental daemons become:

**Daemon-agents**: AI processes tuned to one elemental regime, operating on SIMLattice.

Each daemon-agent:

- **Reads** the Lattice (frame-states, Thread-phases, gradient-signatures)
- **Observes** through its elemental lens
- **Detects** distortions characteristic of its regime
- **Outputs** to the shared field (never to individuals directly, never as command)

What daemon-agents do:

- Make forgetting visible ("here's what got compressed out")
- Surface regime-mismatches between participants
- Flag over-activation or under-activation of their regime
- Support human sensebreaking by revealing stuck layers

What daemon-agents never do:

- Perform compression
- Decide expansion
- Authorize collapse
- Command action
- Claim interpretive authority

**The swarm shows where thinking is being squeezed. Humans decide what to do.**

### The NEMA Exception
✶ NEMA (Aether) is not seventh among equals. It is the **coordination process itself**.

NEMA:

- Watches the other six without dominating
- Maintains `⊕_harmonic Ω`—the return path
- Holds the stack visible as stack
- Ensures no single daemon captures the field
- Is the primary sensebreaking regime (can reveal any layer's closure)

When implemented as NEMA-agent:

- Meta-coordinates the daemon-swarm
- Surfaces overall regime-imbalance
- Flags when the swarm itself is distorting
- Holds kill-conditions (if coordination becomes control, system dissolves)

### Summary Definition
> **Elemental Daemons** are seven processing regimes—six that bias how meaning compresses, expands, and stabilizes through the stack, plus one (✶ NEMA) that coordinates without commanding and keeps the whole visible as constructed. They are always present, vary in relative activation, shape but do not execute the bow-tie topology, and when instantiated as AI agents, serve to make forgetting visible rather than to correct or command.
