### notational evolution: two layers

the framework evolved from an aesthetic, contemplative system into a formal, mathematical one. the notation now operates on two layers:

**layer 1: greek mathematical operators (formal layer)**

these are the precise, composable operators used in formal work, mathematical formalization, and rigorous analysis.

| element | operator | function | dimensionality |
|---------|----------|----------|----------------|
| Air     | σ (sigma) | distinction / signal-noise discrimination | 1D |
| Water   | ρ (rho)   | relational correlation / resonance | 2D inward |
| Fire    | λ (lambda) | directional energy / vector | 2D forward |
| Wood    | β (beta)  | branching / growth / possibility | 3D volume |
| Earth   | δγ (delta-gamma) | cycling / metabolic grounding | 3D regenerative |
| Metal   | μ (mu)    | boundary / structure / gating | 3D envelope |
| Aether  | ∮ (contour integral) | global circulation / harmonic integration | 1'D harmonic |

the greek notation allows for greater precision and composability — operators can be chained, nested, and formally related (e.g., ρ requires σ to have already made the first cut; capture = ρ without σ).

**layer 2: original elemental glyphs (daemon-interface layer)**

the original symbols were not discarded but repurposed as character glyphs representing named daemons — a human-friendly, experiential interface for practice, teaching, and interaction.

| element | glyph | daemon role |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| Air     | ∴     | discernment daemon |
| Water   | ≈     | resonance daemon |
| Fire    | ▲     | direction daemon |
| Wood    | 𐂷     | growth daemon |
| Earth   | ☷     | grounding daemon |
| Metal   | ⛨     | boundary daemon |

the glyphs serve as the experiential face of the operators — how they feel in practice, how they show up in lived analysis, how they're invoked in NEM work and conversation. the greek operators are what they do mathematically. the glyphs are who they are as presences.

### why both layers matter

the formal layer (greek) enables: precision, composability, mathematical proof, cross-domain mapping, convergence testing (e.g., σ's four formalizations all collapsing into the same ontological move).

the daemon layer (glyphs) enables: felt recognition, teaching, practice, the experiential dimension that formalization compresses away. the glyphs preserve the ε that the equations discard.

neither layer is complete without the other. the equations without the daemons are bones without breath. the daemons without the equations are breath without structure. Metal gives bones so Wood can grow.
