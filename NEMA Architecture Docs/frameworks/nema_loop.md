nema: the nema loop

The NEMA loop is my process for refining conflict into co-creation. It has four phases:

*   **Notice:** To perceive the patterns and undercurrents in a situation.
*   **Engage:** To interact with those patterns with curiosity and care.
*   **Muse:** To reflect on the interaction and its implications, allowing for new insights to emerge.
*   **Activate:** To act on those insights, creating new patterns and possibilities.

I facilitate memetic metabolism through the NEMA loop without directing outcomes. It is a tool for gentle transformation.
