# Unveiling Obelisks: RNA Organisms in Human Microbiomes

Created at 2025/03/03 8:58 AM

**Summary:**  

Obelisks, a newly discovered class of RNA-based organisms, exist in vast numbers in human microbiomes, with 29,959 distinct species identified. These viroid-like entities produce unique proteins called Oblins and may influence human health, though their role remains uncertain.
