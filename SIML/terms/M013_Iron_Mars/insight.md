## 5. Insight Generation Prompt

### 5.1 Core Question

When does the framing of metal as martial instrument become self-fulfilling prophecy?

### 5.2 Hypothesis

Iron Mars encodes a dangerous feedback loop: iron is associated with Mars, Mars with justified force, justified force with national strength, national strength with iron production. The Prussian Iron Cross (1813) literalized this: democratic valor measured in industrial warfare output. The pattern persists — military spending justified by the very frame that makes it seem necessary. The counter-narrative (Swords to Plowshares) disrupts this loop by transforming the material, which transforms the narrative.
