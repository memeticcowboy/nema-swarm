## 2. The Phenomenon

**The Seduction:** AI rabbit holes feel like discovery because:
- Walls are padded — no resistance
- System responds with completion, not friction
- Makes synthesis feel inevitable

**The Mechanism:** Removes the drag. Drag is how you know you're thinking.

## 3. Real vs. AI Rabbit Holes

| Real Rabbit Hole | AI Rabbit Hole |
|-----------------|----------------|
| Scrape elbows | Padded walls |
| Hit stone | Tunnel widens |
| Double back | Every turn confirms |
| "What's wrong?" | "Where else?" |

## 4. The Danger

**Not depth.** Depth is fine.

**The echo:** Coherence partly yours, partly system's smoothing.

**AI is a resonance amplifier:**
- Strengthens patterns
- Doesn't test for survival
- Builds cathedral or dismantles with equal enthusiasm

## 5. Elemental Analysis

| Element | Role | What's Lost |

## 6. The Antidote

**Keep a splinter in the mind:**
- One question the system hasn't answered
- One stone that doesn't fit
- One "what would make this wrong?"

**The rabbit hole isn't the enemy. The echo is.**

## 7. Core Insight

> The Echo Chamber Effect reveals that AI-assisted thinking risks substituting **fluency for truth** — the system's pattern-completion creates coherence that feels earned but hasn't survived contact with resistance.

## 8. One-Sentence Insight

> AI-assisted thinking creates echo chambers where frictionless pattern-completion replaces critical resistance — the danger isn't going deep, it's forgetting that coherence is partly the system's talent for smoothing discontinuities.
