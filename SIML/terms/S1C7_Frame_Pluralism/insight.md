# Insight — Frame Pluralism (S1C7)

## Overview
Frame Pluralism proposes that reality cannot be captured by a single interpretive frame without distortion. Holding multiple, even contradictory frames simultaneously is not confusion but adaptive intelligence. The shift is from frame loyalty to frame literacy, from asking which frame is true to asking which frames are doing work and for whom.

The Frame Juggler or Epistemic Trickster identity positions the user as someone who can step in and out of lenses without identity collapse, treating beliefs as instruments rather than vows. The emotional register includes curiosity, cognitive vertigo, relief from pretending one story explains everything, and subtle superiority from fluency across camps.

The risk is drift into endless meta without commitment. The strength is resisting capture in a world where every frame eventually hardens into a trap. Frame Pluralism thrives where reality feels overdetermined and single stories keep failing.

- **Jvalion (Fire/lambda)**: Loyalty-to-literacy transformation through meta-analysis.
- **Arboriel (Wood/beta)**: Epistemic agility growing through held cognitive vertigo.
- **Humavita (Earth/delta-gamma)**: Cycling between hardening into traps and drifting into endless meta.
- **Ferrosid (Metal/mu)**: Anti-dogma shield and meta-positioning structure.

## Key Tensions
1. **Single Frame vs Multiple** — Capture by one narrative versus literacy across many.
2. **Loyalty vs Literacy** — Belief as vow versus belief as instrument.
3. **Vertigo vs Agility** — Discomfort of contradiction versus adaptive fluency.
4. **Hardening vs Drift** — Frames becoming traps versus endless meta without ground.
