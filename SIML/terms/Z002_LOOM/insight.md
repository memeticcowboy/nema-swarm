## Cross-Elemental Synthesis

**Operator Pattern Analysis:**

LOOM operates through ρ (Water) as primary — the recursive integration of repetition into layered depth. This is not mere redundancy but transformation-through-revisiting. Each pass adds density to the pattern, creating the felt sense of "of course" — the inevitability that marks successful weaving.

β (Wood) provides the branching continuity — the loom orients toward what the field already recognizes, using grooves rather than fighting them. This is the generative aspect: fluency as signal that organization succeeded.

μ (Metal) structures the weave as reversible — the boundary condition that prevents groove-lock. The loom that cannot be unwoven is no longer LOOM; it is cage.

**Relation to CONSIDER (Z001):**

⊙ holds the field open; ⟠ weaves it closed. They breathe together:
- ⊙ without ⟠ = paralysis (endless consideration, no pattern formation)
- ⟠ without ⊙ = capture (unconscious weaving, pattern becomes prison)

**Three Deaths of LOOM:**
1. **groove-lock** — ℓ₁ loops without layering; repetition becomes rut
2. **false-inevitability** — ℓ₃ minimizes to a frame that fits beautifully and is wrong
3. **effort-aversion** — ℓ₄ minimizes past productive friction, avoiding necessary difficulty
