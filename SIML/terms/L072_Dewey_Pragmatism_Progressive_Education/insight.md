## 6. Insight Generation

### 6.1 Core Question

How does starting from lived experience rather than pre-defined content transform the nature and purpose of education?

### 6.2 Hypothesis

Dewey's Pragmatism works not by delivering content more efficiently but by **reconstructing the learner's relationship to knowledge** — transforming education from passive reception into active inquiry, from preparation for life into life itself, from individual achievement into democratic participation.

### 6.3 One-Sentence Insight

> Dewey's Pragmatism reveals that education is not preparation for life but life itself — a continuous process of growth through the transactional reconstruction of experience, where learning emerges from inquiry and intelligent action toward democratic participation.

### 6.4 Three-Bullet Expansion

- **What:** A philosophy of education positioning learning as continuous growth through the transaction between learner and environment, emphasizing experience, inquiry, and democratic participation over authoritarian, subject-centered schooling
- **How it works:** Problematic situations trigger inquiry → reflection generates hypotheses → intelligent action tests ideas → consequences transform the situation → experience is reconstructed → capacity for growth expands in continuous spiral
- **Where it aims:** Toward democratic citizenship and continuous growth — cultivating individuals who can think critically, act intelligently, and participate meaningfully in collective life
