# Tree of Knowledge System
## SIML Term Entry S006

## Overview

Gregg Henriques' Tree of Knowledge (ToK) System is a metatheoretical framework that organizes human knowledge into a unified structure. It integrates multiple psychological domains—biopsychology, cognitive psychology, developmental psychology—with systems theory, neuroscience, mindfulness, and wisdom studies. The metaphor of a tree captures how diverse knowledge branches grow from shared conceptual roots.

The system emphasizes interconnectedness across fields. Rather than treating disciplines as isolated silos, the ToK maps their relationships and shared foundations. This enables researchers and practitioners to locate their work within a larger knowledge landscape and identify productive cross-disciplinary connections.
