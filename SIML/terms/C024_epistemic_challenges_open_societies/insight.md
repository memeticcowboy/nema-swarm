## Synthesis: The Discerning Stance

1. Acknowledges the rationality of individual ignorance while working to transform collective incentives

4. Designs institutions that make truth-seeking easier than rationalization

The path forward lies not in naive optimism about marketplace of ideas, but in deliberate cultivation of epistemic infrastructure that aligns individual and collective rationality.
