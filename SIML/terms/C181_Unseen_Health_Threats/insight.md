## 6. Insight Generation

### 6.1 Core Question

Why do the most pervasive health threats remain invisible — and what makes them visible?

Unseen health threats persist not because they are hidden but because they are normalized. Microplastics, job market stress, digital isolation, and sedentary lifestyles are ubiquitous precisely because they are embedded in the systems we rely on for daily functioning. Awareness emerges not from institutional detection but from peer communities (like Reddit) that surface hidden dangers through collective testimony. The act of naming is itself therapeutic — making the invisible visible disrupts the normalization loop.
