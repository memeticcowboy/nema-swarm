## Overview

Metaphysical Memetic Resonance establishes the ontological foundations for treating memes as genuine entities with causal power, merging emergent materialism with informational ontology. Rather than viewing memes as mere metaphors or convenient shorthand for cultural transmission, this framework positions them as cognitive extensions that contribute to distributed intelligence — informational patterns that supervene on material substrates while being multiply realizable across them.

The framework draws on naturalized Platonism to propose the meme-sphere as a natural realm of forms — not a supernatural Platonic heaven but a genuinely existing informational ecology that emerges from and interacts with material reality. Memes in this view are not ghostly abstractions but concrete informational patterns with causal efficacy: they shape behavior, organize cognition, and structure cultural reality in ways that cannot be fully explained by reducing them to their material substrates.

Process philosophy provides the dynamic dimension, emphasizing that memetic meaning is relational and processual rather than static and essential. Memes do not have fixed meanings but acquire significance through dynamic relational interactions between minds, cultures, and contexts. The concepts of supervenience and multiple realizability further ground the framework: memetic states depend on material substrates but are not identical to any particular substrate, allowing the same meme to be realized in speech, text, image, gesture, or digital code while maintaining its informational identity.

## Elemental Analysis

### Air (σ) — Aerunik
The primary distinctions organize the entire metaphysical architecture: individual versus collective cognition, emergent versus reductive ontology, and information versus matter as fundamental. These categorical boundaries determine whether memes are treated as real entities or merely useful fictions, and the framework argues decisively for their reality while maintaining naturalistic commitments.

### Water (ρ) — Sentaria
The relational bridge between personal cognition and collective culture is where memetic resonance operates. Memes are not objects possessed by individuals but relational patterns that flow between minds, acquiring meaning through their connections and interactions. Process philosophy's emphasis on dynamic relational constitution gives this element philosophical depth, showing how meaning emerges from relation rather than residing in isolated units.

### Fire (λ) — Jvalion
The emergent transformation from material substrate to informational pattern with causal efficacy is the framework's central philosophical claim. This is a genuine ontological transformation — not merely a change of description but the emergence of a new level of reality with its own causal powers. The fire of emergence burns through reductive materialism without abandoning naturalism.

### Wood (β) — Arboriel
The meme-sphere grows as an expanding ecology of cognitive extensions, with new informational forms emerging, combining, and elaborating over time. This growth parallels biological evolution but operates in the informational domain, creating an ever-richer landscape of distributed intelligence that extends the cognitive capacities of individual minds into collective cultural reality.

### Earth (δγ) — Humavita
Supervenience and multiple realizability create the cycling dynamic: memetic patterns resonate across different material substrates, instantiated in one medium then another, dissolving from one context and re-emerging in another. This cycling ensures that memes are not tied to any particular physical form but persist as informational patterns that can traverse diverse material platforms.

### Metal (μ) — Ferrosid
The informational ontological structure — particularly the naturalized Platonic architecture — provides the rigid scaffolding for the framework. By grounding the meme-sphere as a natural realm of forms within the physical world, the framework gives memes a definite ontological address without invoking supernatural entities or abandoning scientific naturalism.

## Key Tensions
- **Emergence vs Reduction**: Whether informational patterns genuinely emerge with causal powers or are ultimately reducible to their material substrates
- **Naturalism vs Platonism**: The challenge of maintaining a naturalized Platonism that grants ontological reality to informational forms without sliding into metaphysical dualism
- **Individual vs Distributed**: Whether cognition remains centered in individual minds that share memes, or whether the meme-sphere constitutes a genuinely distributed intelligence that transcends individual cognition
