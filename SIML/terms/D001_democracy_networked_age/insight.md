## 6. Insight Generation

### 6.1 Core Question

How does the shift from representation to resonance transform the nature of democratic legitimacy?

### 6.2 Hypothesis

Networked democracy doesn't just change *how* citizens participate — it changes *what counts as legitimate participation*. When virality replaces procedure and affect replaces deliberation, the democratic subject transforms from rational citizen to resonant node. This is not merely a technical shift but an ontological one.

### 6.3 One-Sentence Insight

> Networked democracy replaces the ballot with bandwidth and authority with authenticity — but in doing so, it risks trading deliberation for affect, and representation for virality.

### 6.4 Three-Bullet Expansion

- **What:** A post-representational democratic form where legitimacy flows from visibility, virality, and affective alignment rather than procedural regularity — characterized by liquid delegation, deliberative platforms, radical transparency, and memetic activism
- **How it feels:** Immediate yet fragmented, resonant yet unstable, empowering yet overwhelming — the thrill of participation mixed with the anxiety of epistemic overload
- **Where it aims:** Toward a distributed, datafied, emotionally charged agora — but with unresolved questions about who controls the flows, who sets the signal-to-noise ratio, and whether freedom is possible when interfaces are designed to seduce, enrage, or addict

## 10. Critical Risks & Pathologies

### 10.1 Attention Economy Capture
The distributed agora may be free in theory, but if the interface is designed to "seduce, enrage, or addict," participation becomes manipulation. The same virality that enables resonance also enables demagoguery.

### 10.2 Digital Divide Amplification
Liquid democracy requires digital infrastructure; epistemic justice requires multilingual, multimodal formats. Without deliberate equity focus, networked democracy risks becoming "code as law" that privileges the already-privileged.

### 10.3 Reality Tunnel Fragmentation
When everyone curates their own information environment, "the will of the people" becomes meaningless — there is no shared "people," only divergent reality tunnels with no bridge.
