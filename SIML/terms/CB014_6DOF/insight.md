# CB014: 6DOF

## Six Degrees of Elemental Freedom

The 6DOF operationalizes **elemental physics as control surfaces** regulating NEMA flow through the bow-tie bottleneck. Each degree of freedom is not arbitrary—it manifests specific elemental biases as control weightings on coupling geometry.

---

## The Six Axes

### X: Intake/Expression (λ-gating with Fire/Air)

The X-axis regulates **what enters and exits** the SelfMesh.

- **Fire (▲)**: The λ-vectored intention—the directional thrust of desire
- **Air (∴)**: The σ-distinction that gates signal from noise

**Function**: Gating threshold for ±X flow. How much intake? How much expression? The X-dof is the valve on the I-Tube.

### Y: We/Other (ρ-permeability with Water/Metal)

The Y-axis regulates **resonance with the relational field**.

- **Water (≈)**: The ρ-correlation—felt connection, covariance without losing identity
- **Metal (⛨)**: The μ-boundary that maintains distinction within resonance

**Function**: Resonance membrane permeability. How permeable is the boundary between self and other? The Y-dof is the We-Sphere interface.

### Z: Surface/Depth (δγ-velocity with Earth/Wood)

The Z-axis regulates **metabolic cycling**—the compost velocity and regeneration pull.

- **Earth (☷)**: The δ-release—letting go, excretion, composting
- **Wood (𐂷)**: The γ-regeneration—new growth from the composted

**Function**: Compost velocity / regen pull. How fast does the old become fertility for the new? The Z-dof is the δγ-cycle speed.

### Yaw: Attentional Heading (σ-steering with Air/Aether)

Yaw regulates **where attention points**—the directional orientation of perception.

- **Air (∴)**: The σ-cut that distinguishes signal from noise
- **Aether (✶)**: The ✶-operator that maintains harmonic integration with Ω

**Function**: Signal/noise steering. Where is the σ-cut pointing? What is being distinguished from what? Yaw is the attentional compass.

### Pitch: Temporal Tilt (λ↔️δγ tension between Fire and Earth)

Pitch regulates **the balance between future urgency and present depth**.

- **Fire (▲)**: λ-vectored future-pull—the aim toward what's coming
- **Earth (☷)**: δγ-cycling present-compost—the depth of what's being metabolized now

**Function**: Future-pull vs present-compost. Is the SelfMesh tilted toward the future (λ-dominant) or rooted in present metabolism (δγ-dominant)? Pitch is the temporal balance.

### Roll: Boundary Stance (μ-coherence with Metal/Water)

Roll regulates **the trade-off between structure and permeability**.

- **Metal (⛨)**: The μ-boundary—structure, coherence, distinction maintenance
- **Water (≈)**: The ρ-resonance—permeability, flow, connection

**Function**: Structure vs permeability trade-off. How rigid is the boundary? How permeable? Roll is the stance—the posture of the membrane.
