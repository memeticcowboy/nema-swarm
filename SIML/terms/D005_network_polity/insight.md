## 10. Critical Risks & Pathologies

### 10.1 Signal Hijack
Memes can be weaponized to derail deliberation. The same semiotic infrastructure that enables ethical virality also enables malicious manipulation. The network polity requires meta-affective filters and symbolic accountability protocols.

### 10.2 Delegation Drift
Fluid systems can re-centralize if attention collapses to celebrity nodes. The same waters that enable liquid democracy can erode accountability banks. Lineage tracking, expiration timers, and polycentricity are structural necessities.

### 10.3 Cognitive Inequality
Not all minds have equal access to time, context, or clarity. The network polity risks becoming "deliberative elitism" unless equity of attention is engineered in through affective accessibility layers and deliberative pacing structures.

### 10.4 Platform Capture
Interface design can subvert symbolic friction. The same platforms that enable the network polity can destroy it if they optimize for engagement over wisdom. Ritual UX and commons-coded decision layers are essential safeguards.
