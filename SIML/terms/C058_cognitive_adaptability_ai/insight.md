## Source Summary
Cognitive adaptability—the ability to learn new things and apply them effectively in novel situations—can be enhanced through AI technologies including personalized training programs, virtual reality environments, and brain-computer interfaces. While offering significant potential for education and corporate training, these tools raise important questions about accessibility, bias, and the risk of over-dependence on technological assistance.
