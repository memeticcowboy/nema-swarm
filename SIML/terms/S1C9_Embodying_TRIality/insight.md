# Insight — Embodying TRIality: Movement Over Definition (S1C9)

## Overview
TRIality is lived through movement, not definition. When insight is frozen into definition, it becomes ground to stand on, defend, or own. TRIality resists this by manifesting in how one shifts frames without betrayal, holds coherence without closure, and keeps understanding permeable to correction.

The transmission model is apprenticeship: learning by watching how elders move, not what they claim. Posture before principle, grace before doctrine. What persists across generations is not the concept itself but the grace with which people move once they have glimpsed it. TRIality survives wherever insight is allowed to remain in motion.

The anti-possession posture means no one "has" TRIality. Closure is treated as loss of practice. The meme resists capture by making possession impossible, traveling through stories, mentorship, and lived examples rather than diagrams.

- **Sentaria (Water/rho)**: Dominant element. Grace over mastery, remaining reachable by insight.
- **Jvalion (Fire/lambda)**: Flowing rather than freezing, permeability to correction.

## Key Tensions
1. **Definition vs Movement** — Frozen insight versus insight in motion.
2. **Mastery vs Grace** — Claiming knowledge versus being reachable by it.
3. **Doctrine vs Apprenticeship** — Teaching concepts versus transmitting posture.
4. **Taxonomy vs Weaving** — Mapping versus living through.

## Source Context
A memetic analysis of TRIality as lived practice that resists conceptual capture, emphasizing embodied transmission through apprenticeship and grace. Connected to anti-ownership epistemology and post-doctrinal pluralism.
