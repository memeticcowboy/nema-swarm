# CB005 — Mythworks

**Elemental Insight: Wood/Water/Meta**

## The Question

How do you test a framework against reality without being constrained by reality?

## The Operation

Mythworks are **laboratories of imagination**. OstrichTech isn't Google. Pinktopia isn't a real place. They're **hyperreal**—more true than literal because they compress the essential patterns without the noise of specificity.

## Elemental Emphasis

**β-Wood (Branching)**: The narrative generates possibilities. Each mythwork is a branch of the framework, testing what happens when it grows in fictional soil.

**ρ-Water (Resonance)**: The emotional truth. You feel OstrichTech in your gut because you've worked there—even if you haven't.

**✶-Meta (Integration)**: The harmonic point where the framework's abstraction meets human complexity's density.

The danger: mistaking the map for the territory. OstrichTech illuminates but doesn't *prove*. The framework might fail in the real world in ways the mythwork couldn't predict.
