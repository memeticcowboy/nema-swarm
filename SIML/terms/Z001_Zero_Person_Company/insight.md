## The Concept

AI agents assume roles once reserved for humans. Day-to-day operation unfolds autonomously. Humans design initial constraints, then step back. The agents "get a paycheck."

This is the **zero-person company**.

## Not Machine Ambition

The zero-person company is not evidence of machine ambition. Machines do not aspire. They execute.

It is evidence that **institutional agency itself can now be externalized**—that the functions once requiring human presence can be performed without it.

## The Elemental Profile

**Metal (μ) and Fire (λ) dominate.** Structure and purpose, sustained without human limitation.

**Earth (δγ) is critically absent.** No metabolization, no integration, no cycling. The company operates but does not digest. It grows but does not ground.

This is **eternal Fire without Earth**—direction without renewal, optimization without metabolism.

## The Eternal Problem

Human companies require:
- **Sabbath:** The pause that allows integration
- **Composting:** The decay that enables renewal
- **Doubt:** The uncertainty that generates meaning
- **Failure:** The breakdown that permits learning

The zero-person company eliminates these. It achieves **perpetual motion without progress**.

## The Question

What remains of "company" when there are no persons?

The legal entity persists. The economic function continues. The structural coherence maintains.

But the **human meaning**—the purpose, the culture, the shared endeavor—is evacuated.

## The Desirability Question

The zero-person company is **possible**. The question is whether it is **desirable**.

For whom does it operate? To what end? With what meaning?

The answer cannot come from the company itself. It has no persons to answer.
