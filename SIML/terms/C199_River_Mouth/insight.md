## Cross-Elemental Synthesis

- **Operator pattern analysis:** The active operators sequence as **λ → σ → δγ**. The **Fire (λ)** operator provides the directed, energetic flow of the river. The **Earth (δγ)** operator metabolizes this flow into a cyclical process of deposition and land-building. The tension lies in the **δγ** cycle being starved by external constraints (damming, climate change), threatening to lock the system into a stagnant, vulnerable state.
