## 6. Insight Generation

### 6.1 Core Question

How does creating psychological safety enable self-directed growth toward full human potential?

### 6.2 Hypothesis

Humanistic learning emerges from **integrative becoming** — when learners experience congruence, unconditional positive regard, and empathic understanding, their natural growth tendency is released. The facilitator doesn't shape the learner but removes obstacles to natural unfolding. Self-actualization is not achieved but allowed — a harmonic integration of all human capacities toward authentic becoming.

### 6.3 One-Sentence Insight

> Humanistic learning demonstrates how self-actualization emerges through learner-centered education — when facilitators provide empathy, authenticity, and unconditional positive regard, learners naturally grow toward their full potential.

### 6.4 Three-Bullet Expansion

- **What:** A learner-centered approach emphasizing the whole person's growth toward self-actualization through intrinsic motivation, experiential learning, and facilitative relationships
- **How it works:** Facilitator creates psychological safety (congruence + unconditional positive regard + empathy) → Learner engages in self-directed inquiry → Meaningful learning emerges → Progress toward self-actualization
- **Where it aims:** Toward authentic becoming — education as a journey of human flourishing where learners realize their unique potential and develop their full humanity
