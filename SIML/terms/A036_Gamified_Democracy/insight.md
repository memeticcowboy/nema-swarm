## Design Implications

### For Democratic Gamification Systems
1. **Measure what matters**: Optimize for epistemic progress, not engagement
2. **Require justification**: Votes must include reasoning
3. **Reward counterargument**: Incentivize changing your mind
4. **Transparent feedback**: Show real-world outcomes of decisions
5. **Iterative design**: Allow policies to evolve based on results
