## Core Insight

Epistemic justice, when analyzed through the Integrated Systemic Thinking Framework (ISTF), reveals itself not as a problem of access or representation, but as a fundamental question of **ontological sovereignty**: who has the right to define reality, and whose cognitive structures get to determine what counts as knowledge?

The 7b variant shifts the frame from procedural justice (adding more voices) to epistemological transformation (reforging the architecture of knowing). This is the soul of legitimacy in a Network Polity.
