# Insight — Reframing Amplitude: From Shame to Insight (S1BA)

## Overview
This entry encodes the reframing of amplitude from moral risk to structural signal. Human systems suppress range of motion, intensity, and scale of becoming by moralizing them as danger. The fall narrative trains fear through punishment. Liberation occurs when amplitude is reclassified: when something breaks, it is evidence of an undersized container, not a flawed motion.

The shift is from "I went too far" to "what ceiling constrained this movement?" The self is no longer a transgressor of norms but a diagnostic instrument revealing load limits in inherited systems. This detaches intensity from guilt and reattaches it to design, using physics and kinematics instead of moral language.

The kinematic vocabulary (pitch, yaw, roll, surge, heave, sway) provides a non-moral framework for understanding human dynamics. Growth is a physics problem, not an ethical one. "Amplitude isn't arrogance. It's information. If something breaks, resize the container."
