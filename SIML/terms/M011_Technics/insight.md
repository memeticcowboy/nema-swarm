If tools make the human rather than humans making tools, what does AI make us?

### 5.2 Hypothesis

Stiegler's insight is that technics is not neutral instrument but constitutive dimension. The human is defined as the being whose relation to its own past is mediated through technical supports that outlast any single life. This means each new technical substrate (print, television, AI) does not merely add capability — it reconstitutes what it means to be human. AI is not supplementing cognition; it is transforming the conditions of cognition itself.
