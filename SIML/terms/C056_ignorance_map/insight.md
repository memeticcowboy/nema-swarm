## Cross-Elemental Synthesis

The Ignorance Map emerges at the intersection of:
- **Rumsfeld's epistemic taxonomy** (Air) meeting **psychoanalytic denial** (Water)
- **Critical self-examination** (Fire) transforming **tacit knowledge** (Wood)
- **Epistemic humility** (Earth) grounding **structured inquiry** (Metal)

This framework does not eliminate ignorance but makes it *navigable*—transforming the unknown from a void into a mapped terrain with distinct regions, each requiring different strategies of approach.
