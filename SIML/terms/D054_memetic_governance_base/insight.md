Memetic governance arrives at **meaning-sovereignty** — a state where "whoever curates the meme-stream curates what is governable." This is not merely about political messaging; it is a fundamental transformation in how collective coordination occurs.

- Affective resonance enables coordination without centralized control
- Symbolic compression allows complex governance to operate at speed

3. **Speed vs. Depth:** Memetic governance operates at viral velocity. Can deliberative depth survive in a memescape optimized for speed and spectacle?

4. **Decentralization vs. Accountability:** Decentralized authorship prevents capture by single actors — but also diffuses responsibility. Who is accountable for harmful memetic effects?
