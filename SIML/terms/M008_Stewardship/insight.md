## The Fiduciary Principle

Fiduciary duty: the highest standard of care in law.
- **Loyalty:** Act in beneficiary's interest, not self-interest
- **Care:** Prudence, diligence, competence
- **Accountability:** Answerable for actions and outcomes

The fiduciary holds what is not theirs, with obligations that transcend personal preference. This is stewardship made legal.

## Ownership vs. Stewardship

**The Owner:**
- "This is mine."
- "I can do what I want."
- "My benefit determines use."
- "I can sell, destroy, neglect."
- Short-term optimization

**The Steward:**
- "This is entrusted to me."
- "I must care for what is not mine."
- "Beneficiary's flourishing determines use."
- "I must preserve, enhance, maintain."
- Long-term flourishing

The owner extracts. The steward **enriches**.

## The Seven Generations
