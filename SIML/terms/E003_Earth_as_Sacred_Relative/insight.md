## 6. Insight Generation

### 6.2 Hypothesis

The Sacred Relative framework is not a belief system about Earth but a different ontological ground. The shift from ownership to kinship is not a change of attitude but a change of reality — in the Indigenous worldview, land loss is not resource deprivation but spiritual trauma because the relationship is constitutive of identity. The practical implication: environmental policy based on resource management will always fail to protect what kinship-based stewardship preserves, because it addresses the wrong ontological level.

> The Earth does not belong to us, nor do we belong to the Earth — we belong with it, and this difference between ownership, subordination, and kinship is the difference between extraction, guilt, and reciprocity.
