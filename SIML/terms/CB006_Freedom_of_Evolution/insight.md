# CB006 — Freedom of Evolution

**Elemental Insight: Wood/Air/Meta**

## The Question

How do you finish without finishing?

## The Operation

Freedom of Evolution is the **discipline of not yet**. The hand stays on the work—maintaining contact, maintaining possibility—without squeezing the life out. It's the β-ethic operationalized: branching, exploring, mutating, but not fragmenting.

## Elemental Emphasis

**β-Wood (Branching)**: The core operation. Hold the form without locking it. Explore without abandoning.

**σ-Air (Distinction)**: The suspension. The form is held in the air, not yet dropped, not yet caught.

**✶-Meta (Integration)**: The permeability. The Ω-hole stays open. The future stays multiple.
