# Insight — IF-Prime SixHats Cyborgs Module (S0F2)

## Overview

The IF-Prime SixHats Cyborgs Module integrates Edward de Bono's Six Thinking Hats with the Six Cyborgs framework, grounding both within IF-Prime's four-domain model of freedom (Head, Heart, Hands, Health). Each Thinking Hat maps to a corresponding Cyborg archetype: White Hat to Analyzer Cyborg for clarity and discernment, Red Hat to Empath Cyborg for emotional resonance, Black Hat to Protector Cyborg for risk assessment and safety, Yellow Hat to Optimizer Cyborg for benefit identification, and Green Hat to Innovator Cyborg for creative generation.

This mapping transforms cognitive hat-switching from a mental exercise into an embodied practice. Rather than merely adopting a thinking style, practitioners inhabit a cyborg archetype — a mode of engagement that operates across cognitive, emotional, physical, and vitality dimensions simultaneously. The Analyzer Cyborg does not merely think clearly; it perceives with the whole body's discernment. The Empath Cyborg does not merely acknowledge feelings; it resonates through somatic and relational channels.

The IF-Prime grounding ensures that freedom remains the central organizing principle. Each hat-cyborg pairing represents a facet of freedom — freedom to analyze, freedom to feel, freedom to protect, freedom to optimize, freedom to create — experienced not as isolated cognitive acts but as interconnected expressions of the body's intelligence across Head, Heart, Hands, and Health.

- **Aerunik (Air/sigma)**: The analytical clarity of the White Hat/Analyzer Cyborg — discernment that separates data from interpretation, observation from narrative, providing the clear perceptual ground for all other modes.

- **Jvalion (Fire/lambda)**: The creative catalysis of the Green Hat/Innovator Cyborg — transformative generation that produces new possibilities from the ground prepared by analysis, empathy, and evaluation.
- **Arboriel (Wood/beta)**: The growth from individual cognitive hat-wearing to interconnected embodied freedom — IF-Prime's four domains expanding personal thinking tools into collective, lived intelligence.
- **Humavita (Earth/delta-gamma)**: The grounding of cognitive modes in embodied intelligence — the cycling between hat-as-mental-tool and cyborg-as-lived-practice, ensuring thinking remains somatic and situated.
- **Ferrosid (Metal/mu)**: The structural architecture mapping each Thinking Hat to its Cyborg archetype — giving form to the correspondence between cognitive mode and embodied function without rigidifying the dynamic interplay.

4. **Individual freedom vs. interconnected experience** — Personal cognitive liberty expanding into collective embodied intelligence
