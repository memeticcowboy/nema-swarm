- **Habitat affinity:** **Pattern itself / Multiscale** — the term has no natural habitat because it is the meta-frame for any system requiring sustained dynamic equilibrium. Cross-currents in every element.
- **Daemon correspondence:** **μ (Metal)** dominates as the structural truth — the Dao as self-containing field within which yin and yang circulate; the lattice that bends, breathes, returns. **σ (Air)** carves the radical AND-distinction (yin AND yang, never yin OR yang). **δγ (Earth)** reveals the metabolic basis — composting is built into the symbol; each pole feeds the next. **ρ (Water)** flows reciprocally — neither pole exists without the other to define it.
- **IF-Prime grammar:** **IF** a system is held in a self-containing frame **AND** each pole carries the seed of its opposite, **THEN** dominance is always temporary, reversal is inevitable, and the system generates novelty through sustained tension rather than resolution.

## Cross-Elemental Synthesis

- **Operator pattern analysis:** The active sequence is **μ → σ → δγ → ρ**. Metal sets the field (the Dao as self-containing frame); Air carves the AND-distinction that holds the pair without resolving it; Earth cycles each pole into its opposite; Water carries the reciprocal flow through the dot-in-opposite. The tension held is between cut and bridge — the same S-curve does both, and the practitioner's skill is refusing to choose.
- **Cross-cultural bridges:** Heraclitus's *enantiodromia* (everything turns into its opposite); Hegel's dialectic (thesis-antithesis-synthesis, though the Dao refuses synthesis as a final move); Niels Bohr's complementarity in quantum mechanics (wave AND particle, not wave OR particle); Carl Jung's *coincidentia oppositorum* (the union of opposites as the goal of individuation); the bow-tie / strange loop topology in systems theory; Spinoza's *natura naturans* / *natura naturata* (the generative process and the generated form as one).
