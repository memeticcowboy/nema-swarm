## 4. The XR Affordance

Extended Reality (XR) creates **presence** — the felt-sense of "being there":
- **Reduces cognitive load** (Air/σ) — immersive context provides natural scaffolding
- **Increases embodiment** (Earth/δγ) — spatial/somatic anchoring of knowledge
- **Enhances motivation** (Fire/λ) — novelty and engagement drive interest
- **Builds self-efficacy** (Metal/μ) — successful interaction in virtual space transfers to confidence

## 5. Core Insight

> CAMIL/XR reveals that immersive learning works not by delivering better content but by **transforming the learner's state** — from detached recipient to embodied participant, where interest, confidence, presence, and clarity converge.

## 6. One-Sentence Insight
