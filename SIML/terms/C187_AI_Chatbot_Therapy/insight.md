# AI Chatbot Therapy
## SIML Term Entry C187
### Source: 1771907212977_1863856_AI+Chatbots%3A+The+Future+of+Therapy%3F.md

## Overview

The emerging practice of using AI-powered conversational agents to provide mental health support, counseling, and therapeutic interventions traditionally performed by human psychologists. Exists in the liminal space between technological promise and human need.

## Depth Analysis

**Surface:** AI chatbots provide CBT-based interventions, coping strategies, and emotional support through text interfaces.

**Pattern:** The therapeutic relationship becomes transactional — users project onto algorithmic mirrors, creating parasocial bonds with non-conscious entities.

**Structural:** A fundamental shift in how society conceptualizes mental health care — from human relational healing to scalable, algorithmic intervention.

**Generative:** The emergence of "synthetic empathy" as a new category of human-AI interaction, forcing redefinition of what constitutes therapeutic presence.

## Key Tensions

1. Accessibility vs. Quality — lowering barriers vs. maintaining efficacy
2. Anonymity vs. Accountability — reduced stigma vs. lack of oversight
3. Scalability vs. Individualization — serving millions vs. unique needs
4. Immediacy vs. Reflection — instant response vs. therapeutic value of processing
5. Cost Reduction vs. Value Recognition — affordable care vs. deprofessionalizing mental health
