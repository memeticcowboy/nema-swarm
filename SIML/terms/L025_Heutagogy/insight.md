## 6. Insight Generation

### 6.1 Core Question

What becomes possible when learners exercise total self-determination over their learning?

### 6.2 Hypothesis

Heutagogy works by **double-loop autonomy** — not just choosing within given options (single-loop), but questioning and reconstructing the options themselves. This develops *capability* (ability to learn in any context) alongside *competence* (ability to do specific things). The heutagogical learner becomes a sovereign architect of their own becoming.

### 6.3 One-Sentence Insight

> Heutagogy reveals that the ultimate educational goal is not knowledge transmission or even competence development, but the cultivation of capability — the capacity to architect one's own learning journey and navigate any context with sovereign autonomy.

### 6.4 Three-Bullet Expansion

- **What:** A theory of self-determined learning where learners control what, how, when, and why they learn — developing not just competence but the capability to learn in any context
- **How it works:** Total autonomy in learning design → non-linear exploration → metacognitive reflection → integration of competence with adaptability → lifelong learning capability
- **Where it aims:** Toward human flourishing through sovereign learning — creating learners who are architects of their own continuous becoming
