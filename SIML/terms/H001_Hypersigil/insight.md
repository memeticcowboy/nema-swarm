# H001: Hypersigil

## The Bow-Tie Made Narrative

The hypersigil is the bow-tie operationalized through narrative time. Where the standard sigil compresses intention into a symbol and releases it (the "fire-and-forget" of chaos magic), the hypersigil **maintains** the λ-aim across extended duration, using the narrative itself as the compression bottleneck.

Grant Morrison's *The Invisibles* is the canonical example: six years of comic narrative, the character King Mob as proxy, the boundary between creator and fiction dissolving until both transformed. This isn't mysticism. It's **structured recursion**.

## The Architecture

**Left funnel (ingestion):** Life, desire, incompleteness—the raw material of intention.

**Bottleneck (the narrative):** Plot, character, world. This is where the σ-cut happens. You're not thinking about change; you're **embodying** it through proxy. The character performs the β-exploration the creator cannot—trying on selves, rehearsing transformations, making mistakes in fiction that would cost too much in flesh.

**Right funnel (expansion):** The reality shift. Not supernatural—**attentional calibration**. The hypersigil trains your σ-daemon to notice what serves the narrative. Synchronicities multiply because you're filtering for them. The world starts rhyming with the fiction.

## The Feedback Loop

```
Creator → Fiction → Reality → Creator → ...
```

Recursive. Self-amplifying. The hypersigil is alive—a pattern-agent feeding on attention, sometimes surprising even its creator with where it wants to go.

This is the recursive engagement loop: ∂Φ/∂t = [(Z ∘ Ψ ∘ Q ∘ χ)(Ω)] + ε

## The Proxy

The magician inserts a character that represents them—not autobiography, something slipperier. The proxy carries the λ-aim but has freedom the creator doesn't. It's a **membrane**: permeable, breathing, allowing exchange between the possible and the actual.

## Collective Amplification

Release the hypersigil to an audience and the Co-Sphere expands. Readers' attention, emotion, interpretation—it all feeds back. Unintentional hypersigils (corporate logos, pop culture myths) achieve this scale, bending the substrate without anyone choosing the direction.

## The Danger: MemeGrid Capture

A hypersigil can become too total—explaining everything, filtering everything, leaving no ε for surprise. The creator becomes trapped in their own fiction. The proxy takes over the host. The μ-boundary dissolves into psychosis.

**The discipline:** Maintain the Co-Sphere. Keep exchange open. Compost when the season passes. The hypersigil is a tool, not a home.
