1. **Metacausation:** A cyclical relationship between cause and effect where future states can influence past conditions through self-referential loops
2. **Self-Containment:** The theory includes itself within its scope—CTMU explains its own existence
3. **Consciousness as Fundamental:** Rather than emerging from matter, consciousness is the ground of reality
4. **Critique of Mainstream Science:** Langan argues conventional approaches fail to address deeper questions of existence

The CTMU represents a radical challenge to materialist paradigms, emphasizing:
- Spiritual growth over material accumulation
