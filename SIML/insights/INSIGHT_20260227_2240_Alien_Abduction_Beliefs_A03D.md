### ⛨ Metal — The Structure-Guardian
> *What structures would distinguish genuine memory from confabulation? What boundaries protect against source monitoring errors?*

Metal examines the epistemic architecture: how do we know what we remember? Source monitoring—the cognitive system that tags memories with their origins (saw in movie vs. happened to me)—can fail, especially under conditions of high absorption, dissociation, or suggestion. What structures would prevent such errors? Rigorous documentation, corroborating evidence, awareness of memory's constructive nature. The tragedy: the very psychological traits that make abduction beliefs possible (absorption, magical ideation) also make source monitoring more difficult. Metal holds the boundary between what can be known and what must remain uncertain.
