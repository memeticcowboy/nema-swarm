## Core Concept
Status Quo Bias is the preference for the current state of affairs, rooted in loss aversion and fear of regret. It manifests as resistance to change even when alternatives offer clear benefits.

## Overcoming Status Quo Bias

1. **Active Consideration:** Deliberately seek out and evaluate alternatives
2. **Periodic Reassessment:** Regularly review choices without defaulting to current state
3. **Growth Mindset:** Cultivate openness to change as opportunity
4. **Loss Reframing:** Consider what is lost by *not* changing
5. **Cultural Awareness:** Recognize how cultural values influence bias strength
