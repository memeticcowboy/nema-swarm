# C087: Cognitive Biases Quirks — Elemental Emphasis Questions

## ∴ Air (σ) — Classification Questions
- How do we classify the types of cognitive biases? What categorical cuts separate memory biases, decision-making flaws, information overload effects, and pattern-seeking errors?
- What analytical distinctions reveal the difference between valid pattern recognition and false pattern imposition (apophenia)?
- How do we categorize biases by cognitive domain: memory, decision-making, information processing, and meaning-making?

## ≈ Water (ρ) — Relational Flow Questions
- What flows between distorted perception and flawed decision? How does the quality of awareness shape the accuracy of judgment?
- What relational dynamics connect confirmation bias to echo chambers and polarization?
- How do diverse perspectives serve as channels to bridge individual blind spots?
- What is the relationship between information overload and decision paralysis?

## ▲ Fire (λ) — Transformative Spark Questions
- Where does the spark of pressure ignite biased judgment? What transforms stress into opportunities for mindful deliberation?
- How does time pressure catalyze heuristic overreliance?
- What transforms the discomfort of cognitive dissonance into growth versus defensiveness?
- Where does the recognition of one's own biases ignite corrective action?

## 𐂷 Wood (β) — Developmental Growth Questions
- What forms of awareness growth emerge from bias recognition? What developmental potential lies in cultivating metacognitive vigilance?
- How does repeated practice at bias detection develop into automatic vigilance?
- What is the developmental progression from unconscious bias operation to conscious bias management?
- How do we grow our capacity to hold uncertainty without rushing to false clarity?

## ☷ Earth (δγ) — Grounding Questions
- What sustains the ground of empirical reality against distortion? What are the cyclical returns between simplified models and complex truth?
- How do we anchor perception in evidence rather than convenience?
- What practices ground us in accepting ambiguity and resisting apophenia?
- How do we cycle between confident judgment and humble revision?

## ⛨ Metal (μ) — Boundary Questions
- What structures contain bias boundaries? What thresholds separate helpful heuristics from harmful distortions?
- How do we construct frameworks that catch biases before they affect decisions?
- What boundaries distinguish efficient mental shortcuts from systematic errors?
- How do we recognize the threshold where awareness triggers corrective intervention?
