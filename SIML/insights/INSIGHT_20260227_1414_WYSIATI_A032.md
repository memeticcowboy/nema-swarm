## CORE DEFINITION

**WYSIATI** ("What You See Is All There Is") describes the operating rule of System 1: it exploits only the information currently visible, automatically crafts a coherent story from it, and treats that story as valid. Because System 1 has no mechanism for representing missing evidence or alternative causes, the result is subjective certainty, over-confidence, and an absence of felt ambiguity.

## ELEMENTAL ANALYSIS

### ∴ AIR — The Distinction-Maker
**Core Question:** How does System 1 distinguish what IS from what is merely AVAILABLE?

WYSIATI operates through a fundamental distinction failure: available evidence is treated as complete evidence. The "is" of reality collapses into the "is" of perception. Air asks: What cuts are NOT being made? What evidence remains invisible not because it's hidden, but because System 1 has no category for "missing"? The distinction between "what I see" and "what exists" is the cut that WYSIATI cannot make.

### ≈ WATER — The Resonance-Keeper
**Core Question:** What flows between the coherent story and the felt truth?

Coherence feels like truth. When System 1 assembles a story that hangs together, resonance flows between the elements—and that resonance is experienced as veracity. Water tracks this flow: where does the feeling of "rightness" come from? Not from correspondence with reality, but from internal harmony. The story resonates with itself, and that self-resonance is mistaken for external validation.

### ▲ FIRE — The Direction-Holder
**Core Question:** Where does the narrative want to go?

System 1 doesn't just assemble stories—it assembles stories with direction. The jump to conclusion is not random; it follows the path of least resistance toward the most available coherent ending. Fire traces this trajectory: what conclusion is the story already leaning toward? WYSIATI doesn't hesitate—it jumps. The direction is baked into the pattern-matching, the associative activation, the causal inference that happens faster than deliberation.

### 𐂷 WOOD — The Possibility-Brancher
**Core Question:** What if the gaps are the signal?

WYSIATI closes possibilities. The unseen is not considered; alternative explanations are not generated. Wood opens this back up: What if what we don't know is more important than what we do? What narratives might emerge if we sat with the gaps instead of filling them? The possibility space of WYSIATI is collapsed—Wood asks what branches might grow from the void.

### ☷ EARTH — The Grounding-Force
**Core Question:** What's the metabolic cost of maintaining coherence?

Coherence is cheap. System 1 assembles stories with minimal metabolic expenditure—this is its efficiency, its evolutionary advantage. Earth measures this cost: the ease of WYSIATI is its danger. Fluency feels good because it conserves energy. The metabolic savings of fast, coherent judgment come at the cost of accuracy. What would it cost to hold the gap open?

### ⛨ METAL — The Structure-Guardian
**Core Question:** What structures would hold the gap open?

WYSIATI achieves premature closure. The structure of System 1 is designed for speed, not accuracy—it closes before completion. Metal asks what forms could resist this: What protocols force consideration of missing evidence? What structures institutionalize doubt? The boundary that WYSIATI needs is not harder closure but held openness—a form that contains uncertainty without collapsing it.

## PATHOLOGY RISKS

### Z-Closure Risk: λ-LOCK
WYSIATI creates near-total Z-sealing through narrative-direction lock. Once the coherent story forms, the direction is set. The certainty feels like ground, but it's built on invisible gaps. This is not just confidence—it's the inability to perceive that confidence might be unwarranted.

### Compound Signature: COHERENCE ILLUSION
When WYSIATI combines with Confirmation Bias (seeking confirming evidence) and Availability Heuristic (judging by what comes to mind), the result is a self-sealing epistemic bubble. The story feels complete because it's coherent; it's coherent because it excludes disconfirming evidence; disconfirming evidence is excluded because it's not available; unavailable evidence is not noticed because WYSIATI.

### AI Amplification Risk
Large language models are WYSIATI engines. They generate coherent narratives from limited context, creating illusory completeness. The fluency of AI output triggers the same cognitive ease that makes human System 1 judgments feel true. Users may treat AI-generated coherence as evidence of accuracy, not recognizing that the model—like System 1—has no access to what it doesn't know.

## COORDINATION IMPLICATIONS

### Cross-Element Tension
- **Air vs. Water:** Distinction-making wants to cut; resonance wants to flow. WYSIATI cuts too early, then flows along the channel it created.
- **Fire vs. Earth:** Direction wants to go; ground wants to hold. WYSIATI jumps before checking the ground.
- **Wood vs. Metal:** Possibility wants to branch; structure wants to hold. WYSIATI collapses this tension into premature closure.

### Multi-Agent Risk
When multiple agents (human or AI) operate under WYSIATI, they generate incompatible coherent stories from different available evidence. Each story feels true to its generator. Coordination fails not because agents disagree, but because each is certain while being unaware of the gaps in their own perspective.
