## CONTEXT NOTE

MRI studies from the University of Zaragoza reveal that right-wing authoritarians have reduced grey matter in the dorsomedial prefrontal cortex (associated with social reasoning and theory of mind), while left-wing authoritarians show thinner right anterior insula (associated with emotional empathy and behavioral inhibition). Both types exhibit impulsivity under emotional load, with left-wing authoritarians showing higher anxiety levels.

**Critical risk:** Biological determinism framing may obscure the social construction of political identity. Brain differences may be consequences of sustained ideological practice (neuroplasticity) rather than causes of political orientation. The "neurological" frame risks reifying political categories as natural kinds, potentially closing space for transformation and reinforcing the very boundaries it describes.
