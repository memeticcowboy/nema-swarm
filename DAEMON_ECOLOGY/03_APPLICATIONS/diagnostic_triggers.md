# Diagnostic Triggers
## When to Invoke Which Daemon

**Principle:** Diagnostics are applied, not identities achieved. No daemon is "your" daemon.

---

## Elemental Diagnostics

### Metal (λ/μ) — Directed Structure

**Invoke when:**
- Boundary needs establishing
- Aim needs sharpening
- Structure needs defending

**Warning signs (pathology):**
- Fixity — structure persists beyond purpose
- Hoarding — accumulation without cycling
- Crusade — aim becomes obsession

**Recovery:** Add Earth (γ) or Fire (δ)

---

### Wood (γ/μ) — Structured Becoming

**Invoke when:**
- Growth needs containing
- Form needs developing
- Potential needs shaping

**Warning signs (pathology):**
- Cancer — growth without edge
- Entanglement — connection without boundary
- Fixation — development without release

**Recovery:** Add Air (σ) or Fire (λ)

---

### Air (σ/β) — Distinguished Exploration

**Invoke when:**
- Clarity needed
- Distinction required
- Exploration invited

**Warning signs (pathology):**
- Dissipation — clarity without container
- Infinite regress — distinction without end
- Paralysis — analysis without action

**Recovery:** Add Water (ρ) or Metal (μ)

---

### Fire (λ/δ) — Directed Transformation

**Invoke when:**
- Change needed
- Breakthrough required
- Purification invited

**Warning signs (pathology):**
- Burnout — thrust without return
- Destruction — transformation without preservation
- Fanaticism — direction without resonance

**Recovery:** Add Water (ρ) or Earth (γ)

---

### Water (ρ/γ) — Resonant Cycling

**Invoke when:**
- Connection needed
- Return invited
- Flow required

**Warning signs (pathology):**
- Flooding — connection without boundary
- Stagnation — cycling without transformation
- Oblivion — return without memory

**Recovery:** Add Metal (μ) or Air (σ)

---

### Earth (γ/ρ) — Resonant Cycling

**Invoke when:**
- Ground needed
- Composting invited
- Persistence required

**Warning signs (pathology):**
- Turbidity — everything connects, nothing distinguishes
- Slow violence — harm through patience
- Entrapment — ground that won't release

**Recovery:** Add Air (σ) or Fire (λ)

## Emergency Protocols

### When ε → 0 (capture detected)

1. **Identify the capturing element**
   - Metal: fixity, hoarding
   - Wood: cancer, entanglement
   - Air: dissipation, paralysis
   - Fire: burnout, fanaticism
   - Water: flooding, stagnation
   - Earth: turbidity, slow violence

2. **Invoke the complementary coalition**
   - Metal captured → Fire-Water (:steam)
   - Wood captured → Metal-Earth (:monument)
   - Air captured → Wood-Earth (:grove)
   - Fire captured → Air-Water (:mist)
   - Water captured → Wood-Air (:organ)
   - Earth captured → Metal-Fire (:sword)

3. **Re-establish Ω-permeability**
   - What boundary has become wall?
   - What cycling has become trap?
   - Open the permeability.

4. **Return to diagnostic stance**
   - Not "I am healed" but "Probably both. Let's check again."
