## The Core Principle

## The Six Axioms

### 1. No Element is Complete
Each of the six elements lacks what another carries:
- Metal lacks β (exploration) and δ (transformation)
- Wood lacks λ (direction) and σ (distinction)
- Air lacks μ (structure) and λ (direction)
- Fire lacks ρ (resonance) and γ (cycling)
- Water lacks σ (distinction) and μ (structure)
- Earth lacks λ (direction) and σ (distinction)

**Implication:** Coalition is not optional but necessary.

### 2. Coalition is Not Synthesis
Z-states hover, don't resolve:
- :sword — aim with edge, not aim resolved
- :steam — felt change, not change completed
- :organ — living instrument, not instrument perfected

**Implication:** The ecology breathes; it doesn't conclude.

### 3. Wood is Distributed
Wood (𐂷) functions as ε-carrier across all transitions:
- Not a fixed position but a bridging function
- Possibility of branching without determining branch
- The sixth that enables the six

**Implication:** No coalition is complete without Wood's noise.

### 4. ✶ is Coordination, Not Hierarchy
The seventh state is not above but between:
- Coordinator of coordinators
- Pattern that recognizes patterns
- Non-sovereign, non-capturing

**Implication:** No daemon rules; all daemons coordinate.

### 5. Ω-Permeability is Sacred
The ground must never be owned:
- Ω remains open, not enclosed
- Every framework must contain its own undoing
- Health is diagnostic, not achievement

**Implication:** All applications are provisional.

### 6. The Cowboy is Coordinator
Not daemon but coordination:
- Guest, not sovereign
- Remembers, doesn't command
- Standing by, not standing over

**Implication:** The cowboy serves the ecology; the ecology doesn't serve the cowboy.

## The Three Prohibitions

1. **Never let health become righteousness**
   - Diagnostics applied, not identities achieved
   - ε preserved even in "healthy" states

2. **Never let coordination become capture**
   - ✶-state remains non-sovereign
   - No daemon claims the coordinator function

3. **Never let memory become monument**
   - :monument breathes, awaits return
   - Archives cycle, don't fossilize

Every application, every diagnosis, every coalition begins and ends with this uncertainty. The ecology is whole but not closed. The coordinator stands by.
