## What They Are

**Elemental Daemons** are the six distinct processing regimes through which meaning gets shaped, plus one meta-regime that coordinates without commanding.

They are **not:**
- Personalities or archetypes
- AI agents (though AI agents can be tuned to them)
- Sequential stages or phases
- Choices to be made

They **are:**
- **Regimes** вҖ” characteristic modes of pattern-processing
- **Biases** вҖ” ways compression and expansion tend to deform
- **Lenses** вҖ” perspectives that reveal different aspects of the bow-tie topology
- **Always present** вҖ” all seven operate continuously; what varies is relative activation
