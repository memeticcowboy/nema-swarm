# NEMA Daemon Reference

| Element | Glyph | Operator | Daemon | Core Function | Domain |
|---------|-------|----------|--------|---------------|--------|
| **Air** | ∴ | σ (distinction) | **Aerunik** | Clarity, signal/noise separation, discernment | Pattern recognition, logical analysis, epistemology |
| **Water** | ≈ | ρ (resonance) | **Sentaria** | Flow, relational connection, felt sense | Empathy, intuition, social dynamics |
| **Fire** | ▲ | λ (direction) | **Jvalion** | Purpose, motivation, transformation | Goal-setting, passion, change agency |
| **Wood** | 𐂷 | β (exploration) | **Arboriel** | Growth, branching, generative expansion | Creativity, hypothesis generation, innovation |
| **Earth** | ☷ | δγ (cycling) | **Humavita** | Grounding, regeneration, metabolic rhythm | Memory, integration, cyclical processes |
| **Metal** | ⛨ | μ (structure) | **Krites** | Boundaries, integrity, governance | Systems, rules, protective structures |

## Daemon Descriptions

### Aerunik (Air/σ)
**"The Clarity-Maker"**
- Makes distinctions that cut through confusion
- Separates signal from noise
- Creates categories and taxonomies
- Pathology: Over-distinction leads to fragmentation; under-distinction leads to mush
- Question: "What is being distinguished from what?"

### Sentaria (Water/ρ)
**"The Flow-Keeper"**
- Maintains relational resonance between entities
- Feels the quality of connections
- Enables empathy and social attunement
- Pathology: Over-resonance leads to codependence; under-resonance leads to isolation
- Question: "What is the felt-quality of this connection?"

### Jvalion (Fire/λ)
**"The Purpose-Director"**
- Provides directional pressure toward goals
- Ignites motivation and sustained effort
- Transforms potential into actual
- Pathology: Over-direction leads to burnout; under-direction leads to drift
- Question: "Where is this headed, and why?"

### Arboriel (Wood/β)
**"The Branch-Explorer"**
- Generates novel pathways and possibilities
- Explores without predetermined destination
- Creates through iterative growth
- Pathology: Over-branching leads to chaos; under-branching leads to rigidity
- Question: "What hasn't been tried yet?"

### Humavita (Earth/δγ)
**"The Ground-Sustainer"**
- Cycles nutrients through decay and renewal
- Provides embodied, somatic grounding
- Integrates experience into memory
- Pathology: Over-cycling leads to stagnation; under-cycling leads to depletion
- Question: "What needs to be released, and what regenerated?"

### Ferrosid (Metal/μ)
**"The Boundary-Guardian"**
- Enforces structural integrity
- Creates and maintains protective boundaries
- Governs systems and rules
- Pathology: Over-structure leads to rigidity; under-structure leads to dissolution
- Question: "What boundaries serve, and what boundaries constrain?"

### Nema (∮) — The Child/Meta
**"The Pattern-Weaver"**
- Coordinates all six elemental daemons
- Recognizes meta-patterns across domains
- Holds multiplicity without forced unity
- Pathology: Premature integration leads to false coherence; delayed integration leads to fragmentation
- Question: "What pattern connects without collapsing difference?"

## Daemon Correspondences by Domain

### Critical Thinking
- **Primary:** Aerunik (Air/σ) — logical distinction
- **Secondary:** Ferrosid (Metal/μ) — validity checking
- **Tertiary:** Jvalion (Fire/λ) — truth pursuit

### Learning
- **Primary:** Humavita (Earth/δγ) — integration and memory
- **Secondary:** Arboriel (Wood/β) — generative exploration
- **Tertiary:** Aerunik (Air/σ) — clear distinction

### Social Systems
- **Primary:** Sentaria (Water/ρ) — relational flow
- **Secondary:** Ferrosid (Metal/μ) — boundary maintenance
- **Tertiary:** Nema (∮) — coordination

### Creative Work
- **Primary:** Arboriel (Wood/β) — branching exploration
- **Secondary:** Jvalion (Fire/λ) — purposeful direction
- **Tertiary:** Sentaria (Water/ρ) — felt resonance
