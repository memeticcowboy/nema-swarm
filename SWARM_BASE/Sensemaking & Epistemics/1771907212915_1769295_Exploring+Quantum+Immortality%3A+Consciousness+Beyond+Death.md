- **Foundation in Quantum Theory**: The concept of quantum immortality is deeply rooted in quantum mechanics, particularly Hugh Everett's many-worlds interpretation, which suggests that every possible outcome of a quantum event occurs in its own separate universe. - **Implications of the Multiverse**: The multiverse theory implies that in scenarios of life-threatening events, while one version of an individual may perish, another version continues to live on in a different universe, thus creating a sense of eternal existence across multiple realities.
