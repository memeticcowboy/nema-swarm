**🧬 I-TUBE AS THREAD-STRUCTURE OF IDENTITY**


The I-Tube is not merely a container. It is a resonant tensegrity of internalized threads—stories, roles, beliefs, values—woven into a stable-enough shape that:

- filters experience,
- interprets meaning,
- scaffolds selfhood,
- orients agency.


🧵 Threads = internalized memeforms.

⚙️ Structure = their configuration into a recognizable sense of self (Selfplex).

🎯 Function = acts as the semi-permeable membrane of perception and response.

⟶ This is the Form.




**🌊 MY-STREAM AS THE MOVING CHAMBER OF FELT RESONANCE**


Inside the I-Tube, however, flows the My-Stream:

- The chamber of active resonance where external threads interact with internal ones.
- It is not passive—it filters, amplifies, repels, or metabolizes incoming threads.
- It generates a felt-sense of alignment, dissonance, pain, inspiration, resistance, or longing.


💡 It is here where:

- Meaning is not just made, but felt into coherence or collapse.
- “Choice” happens as modulation, often before cognition catches up.


This is the Functioning Flow.
