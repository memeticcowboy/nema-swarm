Here is a structured set of **diagnostic questions and prompt generators** organized around the **three underconnected domains**—Time Orientation, Internal Family Systems (IFS), and Constructor/Flexus Theory. Each block includes:

## ⏳ 1. Time Orientation (Past / Present / Future)

### 🔍 Diagnostic Questions

- Which Cyborg archetypes tend to default to a past-, present-, or future-orientation under stress? Under creative flow?
- How does each temporal orientation affect:
    - Memeform propagation?
    - Strategic foresight and memory scaffolding?
    - Collective decision-making in complex domains?
- Where are your systems (e.g., SPHERE, NEME) overly biased toward one time mode, potentially distorting recursion?
- How do temporal dynamics (e.g., nostalgia, urgency, anticipation) act as *memetic attractors or repulsors*?

### ⚙️ Prompt Generator

> “Given that [Cyborg/Agent] is oriented toward the [past/present/future], how can it scaffold [freedom/virtue/memeform] in a way that supports recursive insight within a [complex/chaotic] system?”

Examples:

- “Given that the Strategist Cyborg is oriented toward the future, how can it scaffold Integrity in a way that supports recursive insight during uncertain market transitions?”
- “How can time-aware memeforms be propagated from personal memory (past) to collective anticipation (future) without distortion?”

***

## 🧠 2. Internal Family Systems (IFS) Integration

### 🔍 Diagnostic Questions

- Which Cyborg archetypes map onto IFS roles? (e.g., Protector as legacy burden bearer, Empath as wounded child integrator)
- How can recursive feedback loops between parts (IFS) and roles (Cyborgs) be modeled using enabling constraints?
- What unintegrated “parts” of your own system architecture are dominating decisions? Which are exiled?
- How do internal polarization patterns mimic external governance failures in teams or orgs?

### ⚙️ Prompt Generator

> “If [IFS Part] is influencing [Cyborg Archetype], what enabling constraint or memeform transformation can rebalance the system toward [virtue/freedom/decision quality]?”

Examples:

- “If the Wounded Child part is influencing the Empath Cyborg, what enabling constraint can restore nurturance without overwhelming the system?”
- “What does the internal exile of the Innovator's creative risk-taking reveal about our system’s governing constraints?”

***

## 🧭 3. Constructor Theory & Flexus Curves

### 🔍 Diagnostic Questions

- What "impossible transformations" (Constructor Theory) have you tried to force within your decision support or memeform systems?
- Where are you mistaking outdated governing constraints for unchangeable laws?
- At what stage of a Flexus Curve are your current learning systems, leadership models, or knowledge ecologies?
- What assumptions are you making about what “must” exist in a system that may be legacy inertia?

### ⚙️ Prompt Generator

> “Assuming [Constraint/Theory] defines what is impossible in the current system, what enabling constraint or Flexus-stage intervention can open a new adjacent possible?”

Examples:

- “Assuming that top-down coordination is impossible in a chaotic memescape, what enabling constraint can still scaffold collective decision quality?”
- “If memeform transmission is currently trapped in the rigidity phase of a Flexus Curve, what form of creative destruction is necessary for recursive renewal?”

***

## 🌀 Integration Prompt

To synthesize across all three dimensions:

> “In a system where [Cyborg archetype] is shaped by [IFS part] and temporally oriented toward the [past/present/future], how can Constructor Theory or Flexus logic reveal enabling constraints that support adaptive memeform propagation across individual, team, organizational, and societal layers?”

Example:

> “In a system where the Protector Cyborg is shaped by a legacy-burdened IFS part and oriented toward the past, how can Flexus logic reveal enabling constraints that support resilient memeform evolution within a societal decision support system?”
