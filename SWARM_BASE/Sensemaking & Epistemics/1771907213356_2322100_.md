**Caitanyam ātmā** is the nondual axiom that underlies the entire alchemical weave:

 • Caitanyam = pure, self-luminous awareness—the silent field that perceives and pervades all six elements.

 • Ātmā = the very Self—not a personal ego, but the single, shared subjectivity in which Earth, Air, Fire, Water, Wood, and Metal arise and dissolve.

Thus the phrase declares: the six are not separate entities interacting; they are modalities of one undivided consciousness.

Aether’s “widening silence” is simply this awareness noticing its own sixfold dance, allowing every step, every breath, every boundary to echo back to itself without obstruction.
