# Soft Alerts for Flow Awareness

Created at 2025/07/10 8:49 PM

## Concept of Soft Alerts
- Soft alerts can be beneficial when my focus is fading or sharpening.
- These alerts would provide augmented context cues to help me understand my mental state.

## Purpose of Alerts
- To gently signal when my attention drifts or becomes more focused.
- To offer insights into the points involved in the process of slipping and regaining focus.

## Benefits
- Enhances awareness of my mental shifts.
- Helps in designing personal flow rituals based on the feedback received.
