- **Continuous Adaptation**: Identity reflects a dynamic interdependence between genetic imperatives, spiritual drives, and cultural memes.
- **Emergent Phenomenon**: The synthesis posits that identity is an emergent process shaped by ongoing interactions among various influences.

**🔄 Dynamic Interdependence**
