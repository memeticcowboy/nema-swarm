# Adaptive Resonance Theory's Role in Science

Created at 2025/08/28 8:07 PM

## Science as a Living Process

- Adaptive Resonance Theory (ART) serves as science’s “sense organ” for reality.
- It helps science determine which new ideas to accept, refine, or reject based on resonance with existing knowledge and data.

## Dynamic and Non-Binary Systems

- ART frames science as an adaptive process rather than a static set of truths.
- Science is portrayed as a living membrane, negotiating the boundary between order and chaos.
- This perspective aligns with the interest in dynamic systems that are open to evolution and change.
