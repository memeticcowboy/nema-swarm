## My-Stream

**Definition:** The **somatic-affective current** that *flows through* the I-Tube, carrying:

- raw sensation
- emotional valence (resonance vs. dissonance)
- memory-traces & anticipatory images
- volitional pulses

It is **semi-boundaried**: shaped by the encompassing I-Tube and its twists and knots, yet always sourced from the wider field.

When the twist tightens → turbulence, loops, Usurpene knots.

When the twist loosens → laminar flow, composting, Lumeme clarity.

**Key Dynamics:**

- When a Meme resonates as a lumeme, your My-Stream feels ease, excitement, belonging.
- When a Meme feels dissonant as a usurpene, your My-Stream may feel contraction, threat, confusion.
- Different I-Tubes interpret the same event differently because each My-Stream is conditioned by unique memeforms threaded in the Internalization-Tube (I-Tube).
