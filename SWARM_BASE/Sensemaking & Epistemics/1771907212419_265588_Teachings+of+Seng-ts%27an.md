## Insight
Seng-ts'an, the third patriarch of Chan (Zen) Buddhism, is renowned for his poem 'Xinxin Ming' (Faith in Mind), which encapsulates his teachings on non-duality and the nature of mind. His philosophy emphasizes transcending dualistic thinking, embracing emptiness and oneness, and maintaining present moment awareness. These teachings have profoundly influenced Zen Buddhism and continue to be studied and recited in Zen communities.
