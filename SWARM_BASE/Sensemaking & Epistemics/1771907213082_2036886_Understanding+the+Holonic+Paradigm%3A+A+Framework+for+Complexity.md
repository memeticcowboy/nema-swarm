### 3️⃣ **Fundamental Concepts**

- **Holon**: A unit that is simultaneously a whole and a part
- **Holarchy**: A recursive, nested structure of holons
- **Emergence**: Novel properties arising from interactions, not reducible to components
- **Autonomy + Integration**: The balancing dynamic that defines holonic viability
- **Reductionism vs Holism**: Competing epistemologies reconciled through holonic thinking
- **Viable System Model**: A cybernetic model that reflects holonic principles in orgs
- **Integral Methodological Pluralism**: Epistemological stance honoring multiple valid views
- **Memetic Holons**: Units of cultural information functioning within knowledge systems
