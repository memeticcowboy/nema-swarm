# Exploring Actor-Network Theory: Key Readings

Created at 2025/08/01 9:36 AM

## Essential Texts
- **Bruno Latour – *Reassembling the Social***: Core ANT text, reframing “the social” as networks of humans and nonhumans.
- **John Law – “Notes on the Theory of the Actor-Network”**: Clear, accessible introduction to ANT’s main ideas.
- **Michel Callon – “Some Elements of a Sociology of Translation”**: Explores how actors enroll others into networks.
- **Annemarie Mol – *The Body Multiple***: Applies ANT to medical practice, showing how realities are enacted through networks.
- **Latour, Callon, Law – *Actor Network Theory and After***: A collection reflecting on ANT’s evolution and critiques.

## Connections to Memetic Frameworks
- ANT resonates with the shift from ownership to tending patterns.
- Emphasizes distributed agency and the role of nonhumans in social networks.
