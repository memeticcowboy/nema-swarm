# Further Readings on Metamodern Christian Identity

Created at 2025/11/01 10:33 AM

## Relevant Texts
1. **Hanzi Freinacht**
   - "The Listening Society"
   - "Nordic Ideology"
   - Explores metamodernism and meaning-making.

2. **James K.A. Smith**
   - "How (Not) to Be Secular"
   - Engages with Charles Taylor’s "A Secular Age," exploring faith in a post-secular world.

3. **Peter Rollins**
   - "Insurrection"
   - "The Idolatry of God"
   - Focuses on Christian paradox, irony, and the performative aspects of faith.

4. **John D. Caputo**
   - "The Weakness of God"
   - Discusses the event-structure of God and risks of metamodern faith.

5. **Paul Kingsnorth**
   - "The Cross and the Machine" (essay)
   - Reflects on technology, Christianity, and meaning.

6. **Vermeulen & van den Akker**
   - "Notes on Metamodernism"
   - Provides cultural context for oscillatory logic in metamodern identity.

## Themes to Explore
- Oscillation between sincerity and irony in faith.
- Role of sacraments and ancient rituals in modern contexts.
- Active participation versus passive observance in cultural transformation.
