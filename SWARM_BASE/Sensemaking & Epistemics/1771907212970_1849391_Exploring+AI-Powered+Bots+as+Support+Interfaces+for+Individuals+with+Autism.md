Support for Community Inclusion: Peter Jones’s post raises concerns about how to include individuals who can’t participate fully due to physical or mental limitations. Over-Reliance on Technology: While AI bots can be helpful, there’s a risk of autistic individuals becoming overly dependent on them, potentially limiting opportunities for human interaction and social growth, which are also critical for well-being (as noted in ?web:3).

Lack of Emotional Depth: AI bots, while consistent, lack genuine empathy or emotional understanding, which might not fully address the complex emotional needs of individuals with autism. The web results (e.g., ?web:0) stress the importance of human-centered design and ethical considerations in AI for autism care, such as ensuring the technology complements, rather than replaces, human support.
