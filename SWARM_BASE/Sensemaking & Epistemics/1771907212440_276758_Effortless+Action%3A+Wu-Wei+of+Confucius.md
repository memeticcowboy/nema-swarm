**🌊 Concept of Wu-Wei**
- **Effortless Action Defined**: Wu-wei translates to 'effortless action,' a state where actions flow naturally without force.
- **Historical Roots**: Confucius and other ancient Chinese philosophers like Laozi and Zhuangzi emphasized wu-wei as a path to harmony and success.
