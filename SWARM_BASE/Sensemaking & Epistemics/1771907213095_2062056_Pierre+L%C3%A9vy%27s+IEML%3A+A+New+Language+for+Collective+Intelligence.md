- **Cultural and Technical Influences**: The development of IEML is influenced by cultural and technological factors, including the rise of digital data and the need for semantic interoperability. This formalization is crucial for enabling the automatic computation of semantic relationships, thus enhancing the potential for collective intelligence.

- **Interdisciplinary Collaboration**: By offering a common semantic code, IEML allows for the comparison and sharing of theories and methods across different disciplines. This collaborative approach is essential for advancing human development and knowledge management.

- **Addressing Data Challenges**: The vast amount of public data available on the web is often underutilized due to a lack of semantic interoperability. IEML aims to bridge this gap, enabling more effective analysis and application of data in the humanities and social sciences.
