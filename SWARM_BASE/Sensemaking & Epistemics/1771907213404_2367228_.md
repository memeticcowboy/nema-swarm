**I-Tube**


Definition:

The I-Tube is the individual’s temporal tube of attention and meaning-making, where threads (memes) enter, twist, and loop into internalized patterns.

Meta-Memetic Function:

- I-Tube reframes identity from an ego to a dynamic conduit of threads.
- Functions as a meta-meme because it teaches self as a process of patterned flow, not as a fixed entity.
- Triggers recursive awareness: “What threads am I metabolizing?”
- Zone: Primarily I-Tube & My-Stream; connects to We-Sphere when braiding occurs.
