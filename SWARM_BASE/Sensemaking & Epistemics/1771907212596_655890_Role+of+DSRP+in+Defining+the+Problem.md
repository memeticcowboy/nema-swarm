# Role of DSRP in Defining the Problem

Created at 2024/09/24 9:02 PM

## Distinctions
- Helps define what the problem is by setting boundaries.
- Important for identifying differences between various elements involved in the problem.

## Systems
- Focuses on understanding the big picture.
- Recognizes how different parts of the problem fit together and interact.

## Relationships
- Examines the connections between different elements within the system.
- Reveals underlying causes and effects, showing how changes in one area can impact another.

## Perspectives
- Encourages looking at the problem from multiple angles.
- Helps uncover biases or blind spots and consider alternative solutions. 

## Conclusion
- The DSRP framework provides a structured approach to analyze complex problems.
- It serves as a valuable tool for navigating challenges and finding effective solutions.
