# Definition of My-Stream ∿

Created at 2025/08/27 8:38 PM

The My-Stream is a flowing river of internalized, lived experience within an individual. It carries digested meaning, belief-habits, moods, affinities, loyalties, and emotional scripts that shape tendencies, preferences, and reactivity. It is not a static set of ideas but a dynamic current of felt sense of self, often operating beneath conscious awareness. Memeforms flow within the My-Stream, influencing how a person feels and behaves over time. The My-Stream shapes and is shaped by what the individual attunes to and can be repatterned through ecological infoscapes.[1]

- It acts as the integrator of internalized memes, turning them into embodied patterns that influence identity and behavior.
- The My-Stream is the temporal dimension where static memeforms become dynamic lived experience, a living current of affect and meaning shaped by what individuals attune to. [2]
