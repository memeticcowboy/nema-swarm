**🧠 Exploring Integrated Information Theory (IIT) and Consciousness**

This approach emphasizes the importance of understanding consciousness from a foundational perspective rather than as a byproduct of cognitive processes.

- **Challenge to Computational-Functionalist Paradigm**: IIT presents a significant challenge to the dominant computational-functionalist paradigm in neuroscience. By prioritizing consciousness over computational processes, IIT highlights a potential crisis in the current understanding of cognitive functions, suggesting that a shift in perspective may be necessary for deeper insights into consciousness.
