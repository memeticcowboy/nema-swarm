## Lacan’s mind maze: Imaginary, Symbolic, Real!

1. Lacanian Registers of Psychic Experience: Lacan's theory of three interconnected dimensions of human experience.

1.1 The Imaginary:
- 1.1.1: Focuses on *visual and mental images that shape our perceptions.*
- 1.1.2: Encompasses our *idealized self-concept* and ego formation.
- 1.1.3: Originates in early childhood when infants recognize their mirror image.
- 1.1.4: Creates a *false sense of completeness and coherence in identity.*

1.2 The Symbolic:
- 1.2.1: Represents the world of *language, symbols, and cultural rules.*
- 1.2.2: *Organizes* our psyche and mediates our *imaginary identifications.*
- 1.2.3: Enables participation in society through *shared linguistic systems.*
- 1.2.4: Comprises the *abstract structures* that give *meaning* to our world.

1.3 The Real:
- 1.3.1: Refers to what *cannot be represented or understood through language.*
- 1.3.2: Exists beyond our ability to describe or fully comprehend it.
- 1.3.3: Often *manifests* as overwhelming or *traumatic experiences.*
- 1.3.4: Represents the *raw, unprocessed aspects of existence.*
