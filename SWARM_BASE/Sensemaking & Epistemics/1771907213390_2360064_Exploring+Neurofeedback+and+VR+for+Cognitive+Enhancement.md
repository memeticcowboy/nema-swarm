# Exploring Neurofeedback and VR for Cognitive Enhancement

Created at 2025/07/28 3:46 PM

## Interest in Neurofeedback
- Curious about how neurofeedback has worked for others.
- Many report feeling calmer, more focused, and noticing gradual improvements in mood and cognitive clarity after several sessions.
- Results vary; some find it life-changing, while others see only subtle shifts.
- Individual differences and the quality of feedback matter, especially in techniques like Z-Score Training.

## Recommended VR Applications for Neuroplasticity Training
- **XRHealth**: Focused on cognitive rehabilitation.
- **MindMaze**: Offers neurofeedback and stroke recovery support.
- **Cubism**: A puzzle game that enhances pattern recognition and adaptability.
- **Tetris Effect**: Engages cognitive skills through gameplay.
- **Tilt Brush**: A creative tool for immersive artistic expression.
- **Vermillion**: Another artistic VR application that reinforces new neural pathways.

## Studies on VR and Cognitive Enhancement
- A 2023 study showed VR-based rehabilitation improved cognitive function, motivation, and emotional state in post-stroke patients.
- Research on VR “forest bathing” indicated improved emotional well-being and connectedness to nature.
- The Virtuleap Enhance platform demonstrated reliable cognitive assessment and training results.
- Studies found immersive VR training improved life skills and memory in individuals with intellectual disabilities.
