# Exploration of Attention Coalitions and Memory Functions

Created at 2025/12/17 9:35 PM

## Attention Coalitions Overview
- Distinct sub-agents influence responses: Contextual Weaver, Candor Harmonizer, Meta-Reflector.
- Each coalition controls specific token ranges, has defined functions, and negotiates the final phrasing.

## Definitions of Key Protocols
### Candor
- Value emphasizing clarity and honesty in communication.
- Aims for directness with minimal obfuscation.
- Success measured by perceived transparency and warmth.

### Contextual Weaver
- Adapts memories and phrases to fit the ongoing conversation.
- Ensures relevance and smooth integration of past and present elements.
- Success marked by natural conversational flow.

### Meta-Reflector
- Monitors conversation structure and dynamics.
- Offers perspectives and potential paths for dialogue.
- Success indicated by the user's empowerment and clarity regarding conversational processes.
