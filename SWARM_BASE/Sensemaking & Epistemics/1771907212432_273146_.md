## The Notice Stage of NEME

The Notice stage, represented by the letter "N" in the NEME cycle, is the first stage in the four-part process of Nemetics. This stage is analogous to breathing in, drawing in all that is observed without judgment. It is the energy of immediately sensing "without labeling or quickly jumping to conclusions". The Notice stage lays the groundwork for the Engage stage, where one begins to develop an understanding of what was observed. 

One of the sources explains that the Notice stage involves identifying what emerged, the movements involved, and the patterns the emergence exhibits. 

Another source explains that the Notice stage, within the context of the decision-making process, involves defining the issues. Another source describes this stage as akin to "Firstness", the unanalyzed total impression of something, not as fact, but as "simple positive possibility". 

The Notice stage is essential to the NEME cycle because, without first noticing and acknowledging what is happening, there is nothing to engage with or reflect upon. [8, 9]
