## 🧪 **Core Hypotheses**

1. **Egregores as Collective Influences Can Blend with Internal Parts**
    - Egregores (e.g., cultural norms, ideologies) can mimic or amplify internal IFS parts like Managers or Firefighters.
    - Their influence can be unconscious and detectable only through physiological markers, not self-report.
2. **Physiological Markers Can Distinguish Egregore Influence**
    - Egregore-driven states show distinct physiological signatures:
        - Elevated EEG beta (mental stress, control)
        - Low HRV (autonomic stress)
        - High GSR (emotional arousal)
        - fNIRS: high inter-brain synchrony but low individual gamma coherence (suggests "illusion of agreement")
3. **Unblending from Egregores Leads to Measurable Shifts**
    - Self-leadership restoration after unblending from egregoric influence is marked by:
        - Increased HRV
        - Increased alpha/theta power
        - Gamma coherence
    - Linguistic interventions (e.g., “Does this belief feel like your voice?”) can facilitate this shift.
