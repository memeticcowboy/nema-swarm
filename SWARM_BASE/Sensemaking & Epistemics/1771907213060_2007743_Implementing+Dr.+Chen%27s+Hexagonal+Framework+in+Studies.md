# Implementing Dr. Chen's Hexagonal Framework in Studies

Created at 2025/04/15 6:06 AM

## Hexagonal Note-Taking
- Use hexagonal sticky notes to represent concepts or ideas.
- Arrange them on a board or wall to visualize connections.

## Collaborative Study Sessions
- Involve study group members to contribute their own hexagonal notes.
- Create a collective understanding by interlocking ideas.

## Personalized Learning Paths
- Map out interests using hexagons, starting with a central topic.
- Visualize the learning journey and identify areas for deeper exploration.

## Problem-Solving Workshops
- Break complex problems into smaller hexagonal pieces.
- Analyze from different angles to find creative solutions.

## Mindfulness and Reflection
- Use hexagons to visually process thoughts and emotions.
- Connect feelings to studies or personal growth.

## Virtual Reality Experiment
- Create a virtual environment to interact with hexagonal concepts in 3D.
