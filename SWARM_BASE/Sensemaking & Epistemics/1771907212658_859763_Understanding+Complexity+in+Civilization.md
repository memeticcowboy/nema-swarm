# Understanding Complexity in Civilization

Created at 2024/10/30 12:59 PM

The article "Complexity Rising: From Human Beings to Human Civilization, a Complexity Profile" examines how complexity increases across scales, from individual organisms to human civilization. It explains that complexity in biological, cognitive, and social structures results from self-organizing systems. As complexity scales, it affects behaviors and adaptation, with civilizations representing the highest level of complexity due to collective intelligence, cooperation, and cultural evolution. The piece also discusses implications for understanding systemic fragility and resilience in complex societies.
