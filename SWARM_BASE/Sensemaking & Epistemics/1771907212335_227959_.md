Here are some of the top keywords and approaches to use when prompting an AI to enhance your own learning and knowledge:

1. Explain - Ask the AI to explain concepts, ideas, or processes in a clear and concise manner. This can help you better understand complex topics.

2. Summarize - Request summaries of lengthy articles, research papers, or books to quickly grasp the main points and key takeaways.

3. Simplify - Prompt the AI to simplify complicated subjects by breaking them down into more manageable and understandable parts.

4. Provide examples - Ask for real-world examples or analogies to help illustrate abstract concepts and make them more relatable.

5. Compare and contrast - Request comparisons between similar concepts, ideas, or historical events to better understand their similarities and differences.

6. Analyze - Encourage the AI to analyze a topic from various perspectives, considering different viewpoints and potential implications.

7. Visualize - Ask the AI to create visual aids like diagrams, charts, or mind maps to help you better understand and remember the information.

8. Question and answer - Engage in a back-and-forth dialogue with the AI, asking follow-up questions to deepen your understanding of a subject.

9. Recommend resources - Request recommendations for further reading, such as books, articles, or online courses related to your topic of interest.

10. Teach - Prompt the AI to teach you a new skill or walk you through a process step-by-step, as if it were a tutor or mentor.

Remember that the more specific and clear your prompts are, the better the AI can assist you in enhancing your learning and knowledge. Don't hesitate to ask for clarification or additional information when needed.​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​
