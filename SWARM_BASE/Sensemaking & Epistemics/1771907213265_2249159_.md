### **Selfplex** (Blackmore, 1999)

> A “[selfplex](https://www.integratedsociopsychology.net/theory/selfplex/)” is a **memeplex**—a cohesive network of interlinked ideas, beliefs, and schemas—that consolidates around the concept of “I.” It forms our sense of self and identity: who we think we are, what we feel, believe, and aspire to. In this view, the sense of “I” isn’t a singular essence but a structured assembly of cultural and cognitive units (memes).
