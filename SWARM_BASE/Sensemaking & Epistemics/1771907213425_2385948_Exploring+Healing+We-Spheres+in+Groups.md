# Exploring Healing We-Spheres in Groups

Created at 2025/08/10 6:32 PM

## Practical Applications of Healing We-Spheres
- Introduce regular check-in rituals to sense and share the group’s emotional “weather.”
- Create a “Resonance Round” where each person shares a word or gesture reflecting their current state, and the group echoes it back.
- Implement a “Kairos Pause” to collectively pause, breathe, and name what’s present during moments of tension or rupture.

## The Role of Power Dynamics in Healing
- Recognize that power dynamics can block or unlock collective healing.
- Move away from “Power Over” models that suppress voices and rupture trust.
- Emphasize “Power With” and “Power Within” to create a co-created flow where everyone’s resonance matters.
- Acknowledge that even tension can become a shared resource for growth, aligning with the concept of mutual empowerment in community weaving.
