# The Impact of Othering and Storytelling on Identity

Created at 2025/07/31 6:58 AM

## Othering in Recent Conflicts
- **Rohingya Crisis in Myanmar**: The Rohingya were labeled as “illegal immigrants,” leading to dehumanization and violence.
- **Israel-Palestine Conflict**: Both sides use dehumanizing language, making empathy and compromise difficult.
- **Syrian Civil War**: Sectarian labels like “Sunni” and “Alawite” were used to justify brutal actions and rally support.

## The Role of Storytelling in Shaping Identities
- Storytelling acts as a mirror and mold for individual and community identities.
- The narratives we choose can deepen empathy or reinforce divisions.
- Well-told stories can transform social dynamics and foster unity.

## Overcoming Tribal Identities
- The question of whether tribal identities can be truly overcome is complex.
- There is hope in the concept of “post-tribal” memeplexes that allow for belonging without rigid boundaries.
- The future may not be about erasing tribes but making them more open, self-aware, and regenerative.
