## Insight
Let's delve into the concept of integrative thinking and how our mental models shape our perception of the world.

- Mental models are the frameworks our minds create to understand the world, forming our reality over time.
- These models are built automatically and subconsciously, influencing every decision we make.
- Mental models are inherently incomplete and simplified representations of reality, often leaving out details.
- Despite their inaccuracies, mental models are essential for navigating and making sense of the world.

Consider reflecting on your own mental models and how they might be shaping your decisions and perceptions.
