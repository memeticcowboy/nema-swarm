The Tao Te Ching, attributed to Lao Tzu, teaches the art of effortless action, or Wu-Wei. It suggests that striving and forcing against the natural flow leads to counterproductive outcomes. True wisdom lies in harmony with the Tao, an ineffable force governing the universe. Through humility, trust, and non-intervention, one achieves effective leadership and personal fulfillment. The essence of Taoism is living simply, being receptive, and aligning with the natural order, thus allowing things to unfold organically.
