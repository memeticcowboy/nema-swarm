## **S.H.O.C.K.**

**S**ingularities<br>**H**umblings<br>**O**ntologies<br>**C**rucified<br>**K**nowing

Each node gets its own haiku, a snapshot of the tremor beneath the surface—five flashes from the depths of history’s quake-lines:

***

### **S – Singularities**

**(The rupture points—Copernicus, Darwin, AI—where meaning implodes and rewrites itself.)**

> Stars fall inward now,<br>Gravity bends the soul’s path—<br>Time folds on itself.

***

### **H – Humblings**

**(The de-centering blows—"You are not the center", "You are not unique.")**

> Ape with dreams of gods,<br>Watches his throne float away—<br>Dust we were, still are.

***

### **O – Ontologies**

**(The metaphysical maps—re-written, shattered, remade with every cultural quake.)**

> What is real, we ask,<br>As mirrors blur and pulse back—<br>Silence answers first.

***

### **C – Crucified**

**(The death of paradigms—worldviews nailed to the scaffold of new truths.)**

> Kings lose more than heads,<br>Old truths bleed beneath new flags—<br>Dawn comes dressed in ash.

***

### **K – Knowing**

**(What remains—fragile, recursive, uncertain. Truth no longer fixed, but lived.)**

> The truth stares us down—<br>It does not speak, only sees.<br>We blink, then become.

***

This is the memetic terrain where SHOCK is both code and invitation—into the storm, through the collapse, and toward the horizon of reassembled meaning.
