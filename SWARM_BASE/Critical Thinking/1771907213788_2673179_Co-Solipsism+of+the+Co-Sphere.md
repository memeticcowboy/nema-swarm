## Primary Academic Sources by Discipline

### Philosophy of Being & Process

1. **Whitehead, Alfred North.** *Process and Reality* (1929) [web:17], [web:20]

- Gifford Lectures; foundational process metaphysics
- Key concepts: actual occasions, prehension, creative advance

2. **Heidegger, Martin.** *Being and Time* / *Sein und Zeit* (1927) [web:1], [web:4], [web:7], [web:13]

- Division Two on authenticity; concept of Dasein
- Key concepts: solus ipse, thrownness, resoluteness

3. **Deleuze, Gilles & Guattari, Félix.** *A Thousand Plateaus* (1980) [web:2], [web:5], [web:8]

- Volume 2 of *Capitalism and Schizophrenia*
- Key concepts: rhizome, deterritorialization, body without organs

### Epistemology & Philosophy of Mind

1. **Solipsism (General)** [web:46], [web:16], [web:22], [web:25], [web:52]

- Wikipedia entry; Stanford Encyclopedia entries
- Types: metaphysical, epistemological, methodological

2. **Wittgenstein, Ludwig.** *Philosophical Investigations* (1953) [web:47], [web:50], [web:55]

- Private language argument (§243-315)
- Problem of other minds resolution

### Memetics & Cultural Evolution

1. **Dawkins, Richard.** *The Selfish Gene* (1976) [web:18], [web:24], [web:27]

- Cultural evolution through memetic replication

3. **Balkin, Jack M.** "Memetic Evolution" (Yale) [web:27]

- Cumulative memetic development; cognitive filtering

### Systems Theory

- Cybernetic epistemology; systems thinking
- Key concepts: patterns, constraints, feedback loops, mind as distributed
