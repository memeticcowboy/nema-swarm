### Summary:

The Internet and AI influence memory by encouraging cognitive offloading, where people rely on digital tools instead of recall. While some studies suggest technology weakens memory for specific tasks, there is little evidence of broad cognitive decline. AI-driven search may further alter learning by fostering false confidence in knowledge.

### Top 3 Overarching Themes:

1. **Digital Cognitive Offloading** – The increasing reliance on technology for memory and navigation.

2. **AI’s Impact on Learning** – How AI-driven search and automation may change memory formation and confidence.

3. **Debates on Memory Decline** – Conflicting research on whether technology fundamentally harms cognitive function.
