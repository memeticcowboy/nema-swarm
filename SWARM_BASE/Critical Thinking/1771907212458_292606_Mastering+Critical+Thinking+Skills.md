## Insight
This article from Rasmussen University outlines six essential critical thinking skills crucial for personal and professional success: identification, research, identifying biases, inference, determining relevance, and curiosity. These skills are vital for making logical decisions, evaluating information objectively, and fostering continuous improvement. By mastering these skills, you can enhance your ability to analyze complex situations and make informed decisions. If you're looking to improve your critical thinking, start by focusing on these key areas and practice them regularly.

**🔍 Identification**
- **Key conclusion 1**: Identify the situation or problem and the factors influencing it.
- **Key conclusion 2**: Ask questions like 'Who is doing what?' and 'What are the end results?' to get a clear picture.

**📚 Research**
- **Key conclusion 1**: Independent research is crucial for verifying arguments and information.
- **Key conclusion 2**: Develop an eye for unsourced claims and learn to differentiate between popular and scholarly articles.

**⚖️ Identifying Biases**
- **Key conclusion 1**: Evaluate information objectively, like a judge, considering biases from all sides.
- **Key conclusion 2**: Challenge your own beliefs and assess the credibility of your sources.

**🔎 Inference**
- **Key conclusion 1**: Draw conclusions based on the information presented, even if it doesn't come with a summary.
- **Key conclusion 2**: Gather as much information as possible before making inferences to ensure accuracy.

**📌 Determining Relevance**
- **Key conclusion 1**: Figure out what information is most important for your consideration.
- **Key conclusion 2**: Establish a clear direction and make a list of data points ranked by relevance.

**🤔 Curiosity**
- **Key conclusion 1**: Foster a curious mind by asking open-ended questions about everyday observations.
- **Key conclusion 2**: Train yourself to follow up on these questions to deepen your understanding.

**💡 Application in Daily Life**
- **Key conclusion 1**: Critical thinking skills are essential for a successful college career and professional life.
- **Key conclusion 2**: Higher education can significantly enhance your critical thinking abilities.

**📈 Skill Improvement**
- **Key conclusion 1**: Continuous practice and refinement of these skills can unlock your potential.
- **Key conclusion 2**: Use higher education as a venue to develop and improve your critical thinking skills.
