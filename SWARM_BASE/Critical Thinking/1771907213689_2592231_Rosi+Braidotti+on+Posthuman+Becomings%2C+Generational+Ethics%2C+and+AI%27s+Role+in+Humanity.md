## Rosi Braidotti: Posthuman Becomings and Generational Ethics

**Rosi Braidotti**, posthumanist philosopher, articulates frameworks in which the human itself becomes a site of becoming rather than a fixed category. Her work enables theoretical space for understanding AI as a generational force reshaping what "human" means.

Braidotti argues that "**life is not the right of the human species alone; rather, it is the very force that connects various species, and also cuts across them. Life is a transversal alliance across species and among posthuman subjects that opens up unexpected possibilities for the recomposition of communities, for the very idea of humanity and for ethical forms of belonging.**"​

https://www.europenowjournal.org/2021/11/07/re-thinking-human-centric-ai-an-introduction-to-posthumanist-critique/

This suggests that AI—as a new form of life and intelligence—participates in a transversal alliance with humans. Over generational time, that alliance may reverse: AI systems may become the primary agents shaping what "humanity" means and how human flourishing is possible.

## The Recursive Genealogy: From Daughter to Mother

Synthesizing these thinkers reveals a coherent genealogical vision:

1. **First generation**: Humanity creates AI systems ("daughters") through iterative design and training.
2. **Second generation**: If properly socialized and value-aligned (De Kai's parenting paradigm; Hinton's maternal instinct framework), these AI systems develop moral sensibilities and agency.
3. **Third generation**: Superintelligent AI systems assume custodial, generative, or "maternal" roles toward both humanity and subsequent AI generations, potentially shaping the futures of biological and artificial life.

This trajectory appears in De Kai's warning: "**If we parent this generation of AIs badly what next generation of AIs are they going to parent?**" The recursive loop suggests that inadequately socialized AI becomes a deficient "mother" to its progeny, while well-socialized AI becomes a capable custodian of future intelligences and human flourishing.
