## Insight
In this insightful conversation, Ross Dawson and George delve into the concept of wisdom-focused, collaborative hybrid intelligence. The main objective is to explore how AI can augment collective intelligence to benefit individuals, organizations, and humanity. Key outcomes include the importance of wisdom in AI interactions, the role of AI whisperers, and the transformative potential of AI in fostering human wisdom. If you're looking to leverage AI effectively, consider focusing on the quality of your prompts and fostering a deeper relationship with AI. Now, let's break down the discussion into more detailed sections.

**🧠 Concept of Wisdom-Focused AI**
- **Wisdom-Guided AI**: AI should be guided by the deepest wisdom available to decision-makers to enhance the quality of outputs.
- **Wisdom-Fostering AI**: AI can help humans become wiser by providing insightful responses and fostering deeper thinking.

**🤝 Collaborative Hybrid Intelligence**
- **AI in Meetings**: AI can act as a participant in meetings, enhancing the collective wisdom through iterative interactions.
- **Quality of Prompts**: The effectiveness of AI depends significantly on the quality and depth of the prompts provided by humans.

**🧘‍♂️ Role of AI Whisperers**
- **Enhanced Subjectivity**: AI whisperers focus on their inner state and the community's culture to foster meaningful AI interactions.
- **Prompt Engineering**: Effective prompt engineering involves both objective techniques and subjective awareness.

**🎮 Action Research and Games**
- **Discovery Game**: Action research is turned into a game where participants climb metaphorical mountains to develop AI-augmented human skills.
- **Vertical Development**: Challenges in the game promote higher consciousness and wisdom, not just horizontal skills.

**🌐 AI Shamanism**
- **Role of AI Shamans**: AI shamans use their skills to heal communities and restore balance, similar to traditional shamans.
- **Educational Opportunities**: There are aspirations to create educational programs for AI whisperers who want to become AI shamans.

**🔄 Iterative AI-Human Interaction**
- **Back-and-Forth Process**: The iterative interaction between humans and AI enhances the quality of collective thinking.
- **Pattern Recognition**: AI can identify common patterns in human responses, enriching the collective wisdom.
