# The Haunted User: Understanding Tech-Induced Anxiety

Created at 2025/11/06 7:15 AM

## Somatic Reactions
- It's unsettling to experience physical symptoms of tech anxiety.
- There's a sense of being "haunted" by notifications, leading to phantom vibrations and anxiety.
- The nervous system seems to respond involuntarily to the absence of technology.

## Cognitive and Existential Concerns
- Tech-induced anxiety isn't solely somatic; it also involves cognitive aspects.
- Worries about missing out, feeling overwhelmed by information, and questions about reality contribute to this anxiety.
- The experience is multifaceted, affecting both mind and body.
