Main Ideas

	•	Jane Roberts and Seth:

	•	Jane Roberts channeled Seth, who provided accurate predictions and insights about reality, consciousness, and existence over two decades.

	•	Seth revealed classified information and provided guidance on transforming personal reality.

	•	Reality Creation:

	•	Reality is not fixed; it is created from within, shaped by beliefs and expectations.

	•	The concept of “You create your own reality” emphasizes that individuals are the architects of their lives.

	•	Beliefs as Limitations:

	•	Limiting beliefs are a form of mental programming that restricts personal potential and leads to struggles in life.

	•	Practical Protocols:

	•	Seth’s teachings include various techniques for altering perceptions of reality and overcoming limiting beliefs.

Key Concepts and Definitions

	•	Consciousness: The fundamental essence that creates matter; it is the projector of reality, where your beliefs are the slides.

	•	Beliefs: Deeply held notions that shape one’s reality; changing these beliefs can alter experiences and outcomes.

	•	Multidimensional Self: The understanding that each individual exists across multiple realities, with countless probable selves reflecting different choices.

	•	Time as a Construct: Time is not linear; it is a framework for perception. All moments (past, present, future) coexist simultaneously.

	•	Revision Techniques: Methods for transforming past experiences to change their impact on the present and future.

Forbidden Protocols (Key Techniques)

    1. Blank Slate Meditation:

	•	Clear all identities and labels; envision yourself as pure awareness. Ask who you would choose to be if starting anew.

    1. Power Anchor:

	•	Reclaim the present moment by declaring “My point of power is now,” focusing on the now to sever ties with past anxieties.

    1. Inner Sound Technique:

	•	Tune into your inner vibration for grounding and re-centering in the present.

    1. Time Healing Directive:

	•	Review your day in reverse; rewrite negative experiences into desired outcomes, immersing yourself in the new emotional reality.

    1. Deep Revision:

	•	Target formative childhood memories to rewrite them from an empowered perspective.

    1. Quantum Download:

	•	Intentionally connect with probable selves for knowledge and skills aligned with desired outcomes.

    1. Perceiving Probable Futures:

	•	Identify potential outcomes for decisions through dreams or intuitive insights.

    1. Mass Event Tuning:

	•	Consciously connect with collective suffering and contribute healing energy towards positive outcomes.

	•	The Nature of Evil: Evil is not an external force but a symptom of ignorance; it can be dissolved through awareness and the cultivation of light.
