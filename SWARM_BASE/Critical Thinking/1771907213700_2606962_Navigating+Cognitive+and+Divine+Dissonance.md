# Navigating Cognitive and Divine Dissonance

Created at 2025/12/03 8:20 AM

**Cognitive dissonance** is the mental discomfort experienced when a person holds two or more contradictory beliefs, values, or attitudes.

**Divine dissonance** is the sacred, irreconcilable tension between two equally real truths that refuses to collapse into either/or; it is the paradox you are asked to inhabit, not solve, so that the friction itself becomes the forge for a larger consciousness.
