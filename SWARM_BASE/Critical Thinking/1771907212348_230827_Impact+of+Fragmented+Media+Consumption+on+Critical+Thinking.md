## Insight
Let's delve into the impact of fragmented media consumption on critical thinking.

- Studies indicate that heavy multitaskers find it challenging to filter distractions and focus on demanding cognitive tasks.
- Frequent online media consumers tend to have broad but shallow knowledge.
- Digital natives exhibit fragmented thinking and reading patterns, contrasting with past literate scholars.

Consider dedicating focused time for deep reading and critical thinking exercises.
