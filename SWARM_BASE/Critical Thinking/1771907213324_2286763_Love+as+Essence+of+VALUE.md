# Love as Essence of VALUE

Created at 2025/07/05 11:48 AM

## The Intrinsic Value of Love
- Love is never wasted, even when unreciprocated.
- It serves as a bridge between science and spirituality, fostering connection regardless of outcome.
- Viewing love as valuable in itself, rather than a transaction, is liberating.

## Quotes Exploring Love's Value
1. **Henry Miller**: “The only thing we never get enough of is love; and the only thing we never give enough of is love.”
2. **C.S. Lewis**: “To love at all is to be vulnerable.”
3. **Erich Fromm**: “Love is the only sane and satisfactory answer to the problem of human existence.”
4. **Mahatma Gandhi**: “Where there is love, there is life.”
5. **Mother Teresa**: “Let us always meet each other with smile, for the smile is the beginning of love.”

- These quotes emphasize that love’s worth lies in the act itself, aligning with the concept of "Essence" in the VALUE framework.
