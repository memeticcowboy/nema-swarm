## Insight
Let's explore the concept of ignorance through this insightful 'Ignorance Map'. It categorizes different types of ignorance we encounter.

- Known Unknowns: Things you know you don't know.
- Unknown Unknowns: Things you don't know you don't know.
- Errors: Things you think you know but don't.
- Unknown Knowns: Things you don't know you know.
- Taboos: Dangerous, polluting, or forbidden knowledge.
- Denials: Things too painful to know, so you don't.

Reflect on which categories resonate with you and consider how addressing these areas can enhance your understanding.
