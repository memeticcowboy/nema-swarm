**🎨 Sudden Genius**
- **Unexplained Skill Acquisition**: Individuals can suddenly develop exceptional abilities in areas like music or art without any prior interest or training, suggesting a spontaneous emergence of talent.
- **Absence of Precipitating Events**: Unlike congenital or acquired savant syndromes, sudden savant syndrome occurs without any identifiable triggering event, such as a head injury or neurological incident.
- **Epiphany-Like Moments**: The onset of these skills is often described as an epiphany, where individuals experience a profound understanding of the rules and intricacies of their newfound talent.

- **Dormant Abilities in Everyone**: The phenomenon suggests that everyone may possess hidden genius abilities that can be tapped into under certain circumstances.
