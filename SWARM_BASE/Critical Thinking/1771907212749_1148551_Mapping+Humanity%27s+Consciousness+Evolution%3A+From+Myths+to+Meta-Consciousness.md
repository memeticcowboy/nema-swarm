- **Emergence of Hybrid Bio-Digital Consciousness**: The Biotechnology Revolution represents a fusion of biology and technology, leading to Hybrid Bio-Digital Consciousness, where knowledge synthesis evolves towards emergent processes, integrating organic and digital systems.
