**📊 The KM Cognitive Pyramid**
- **Four Levels**: Data, information, knowledge, and wisdom are the four hierarchical levels.
- **Data**: Raw, unprocessed facts and figures form the base of the pyramid.
- **Information**: Processed and organized data that provides context.

**🔍 Detailed Levels**
- **Knowledge**: Information that has been applied and interpreted.
- **Wisdom**: The ability to use knowledge to make sound judgments and decisions.
