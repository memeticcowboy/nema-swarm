# Negative Impacts of AI Use on Cognition

Created at 2025/06/21 8:51 AM

## Cognitive Offloading
- Outsourcing memory to devices leads to laziness in retaining information and critical thinking.

## Attention Fragmentation
- AI-driven notifications disrupt focus, making it difficult to concentrate deeply on tasks.

## Emotional Numbing
- AI-generated content risks losing emotional nuance, resulting in flat interactions.

## Echo Chambers
- Algorithms reinforce existing beliefs, creating bubbles that hinder exposure to diverse perspectives.

## Reduced Creativity
- Over-reliance on AI for creative tasks may diminish personal creativity and the joy of creating from scratch.

## Dependency and Trust Issues
- Dependence on AI can lead to self-doubt in personal abilities and questioning the authenticity of interactions.
