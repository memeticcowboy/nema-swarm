## Part 1: Metal-Wood Diagnostic

### Core Finding

| Element | Core | Essence | Risk | Missing |
|---------|------|---------|------|---------|
| **Metal** | λ/μ | Directed structure | Fixity | β, δ |
| **Wood** | γ/μ | Structured becoming | Cancer | λ, δ, σ |

**NEMAtic Reading:**
- **Metal:** The "sword" logic—cut toward purpose, hold the edge
- **Wood:** The "vessel" logic—grow within form, fill then overflow

**Critical Gap:** Both lack δ (Earth)
- Neither metabolizes cleanly
- Metal hoards (preserves)
- Wood scatters (branches without harvest)

**The Tension:**
- Metal says *"this way"*
- Wood says *"many ways"*
- Neither asks *"what cycles?"* (δ) or *"what distinguishes?"* (σ)
